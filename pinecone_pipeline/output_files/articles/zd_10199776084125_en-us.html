"_connect EACCES" error

This article explains how to troubleshoot _connect EACCES error. 

The error can happen because:

  • You're using a work computer or computer with restricted connection.

         or

  • Your internet service provider is blocking or interfering with Ledger Live connectivity.

    or

  • You're using a VPN that causes connection issues. 

Troubleshoot

  • Try to use a personal computer that has no connection restrictions.
  • Try connecting to a different network or use a mobile hotspot instead.
  • Turn off your VPN.
  • Try using the mobile app instead. The Ledger Live mobile app is available for both iOS and Android devices and can be downloaded from the respective app stores.

Still stuck?

If the error persists, please get in touch with our Support team.
