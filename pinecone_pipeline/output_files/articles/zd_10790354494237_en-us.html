Creating a Ledger MINA account via Clorio Wallet

This tutorial describes how to connect to a Ledger MINA account via Clorio Wallet.

Check the Mina Protocol wallet page to learn more. 

Before you start

  • Make sure to update Ledger Live to the latest version via this link.
  • Open Ledger Live and connect your Ledger device to My Ledger to ensure that your device is running the latest firmware version.
  • Once you're all done, close Ledger Live as it can conflict with the Clorio wallet.

Installing the MINA app on your Ledger device

  1. Open Ledger Live.
  2. Navigate to My Ledger and install the Mina app.
  3. Close Ledger Live.

Connecting to a Ledger MINA account via Auro Wallet

  1. Go to https://clor.io/ website.
  2. Select Use Clorio Browser.
  3. Next, select Log-in.
  4. Select Hardware wallet.
  5. At this step, ensure your Ledger device is unlocked and connected to your PC and the MINA app is open.
  6. In the browser pop-up select your Ledger device and click Connect.
    You should see Ledger device is connected. Click Continue.
  7. Confirm the creation of a public key on your Ledger device. Push both buttons on the screen Generate and then Approve.
  8. Click Access wallet.

Now, you should see your Clorio Wallet dashboard.
