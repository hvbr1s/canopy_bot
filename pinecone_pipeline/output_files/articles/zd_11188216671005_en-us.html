Solve error 0x6b0c

This article explains how to solve the 0x6b0c error that usually happens when you try to manage an account with a Ledger device. 

Your Ledger device may return the following error message:

Something went wrong.
Ledger device: UNKNOWN_ERROR (0x6b0c)

How to solve error 0x6b0c?

To solve this error, try the following: 

  • Ensure your Ledger device is connected and unlocked with a PIN code when you try to make an operation.

Are you still getting errors?

If you continue to see the 0x6b0c error, please contact our Support team.
