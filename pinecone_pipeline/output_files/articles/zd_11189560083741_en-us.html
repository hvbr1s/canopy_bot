How to enter a different billing address?

This article explains how to submit a different billing address during the purchase of Ledger products on ledger.com.

Important note

You cannot change the billing address after you placed the order. The billing address can be changed only during checkout.

Submit a different billing address

During the checkout process on the Ledger website, you have the possibility to enter a different billing address: 

  1. Once you added all Ledger products for purchase, click Checkout.
  2. Enter your information in Customer Info and Shipping address fields.
  3. Click Continue.
  4. Select your Shipping Method and click Continue.
  5. Now, on the Payment Info step, you can select "Use a different address". Enter the billing address.
    This address will be used for billing and can be different than a shipping address.

Want to modify your shipping address? 

In order to update your delivery address, please contact Support before your order is shipped. However, if you need to make the change quickly, it may be more efficient to cancel your current order and place a new one with the updated address.
