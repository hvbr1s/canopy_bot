Bittensor (TAO)

This article provides updated information about Bittensor (TAO). Visit the Bittensor website to learn more about the project.

What's Bittensor? 

The Bittensor team describes the project as a protocol that grants access to the Bittensor network, which is a decentralized ecosystem of machine learning models.

The network utilizes incentives to encourage the development of efficient and client-aligned models, with a focus on optimizing value, diversity, and minimizing inference latency. Various intelligence modalities, such as text completion and causal language modeling tasks, are accessible through the network's API.

Does Ledger support Bittensor? 

No, at the moment, Ledger does not offer support for Bittensor.
