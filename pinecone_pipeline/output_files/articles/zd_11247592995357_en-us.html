Create a Binance Beacon Chain (BEP2) account with your Ledger

This tutorial shows how to create a Binance Beacon Chain (BEP2) account with your Ledger and Binance Wallet.

 Currently, it is not possible to create a Binance Beacon Chain account within Ledger Live. This action requires connecting your Ledger to a third-party application (Binance Wallet).

 Before you go further

The Binance Beacon Chain (BEP2) is considered Binance's legacy decentralized network and is currently not extensively utilized. If you are seeking a tutorial on how to connect your Ledger device to Binance Smart Chain (BEP20), please follow this link instead.

Before you start

  • Download and install the latest version of Ledger Live. 
  • Update the firmware on your Ledger Nano S / Nano S Plus / Nano X.
  • Make sure you're using the Chrome browser.

Video instructions

Install the Binance Chain app on your Ledger device

  1. Connect your Ledger device and unlock it.
  2. In the Ledger Live app, navigate to My Ledger.
  3. Confirm that you allow My Ledger to access your device. You can do that by clicking both buttons on your device simultaneously. 
  4. Head over to App catalog and search for Binance Chain.
  5. Click Install.

Connect your Binance Beacon Chain (BEP2) account with your Ledger

  1. Open Chrome and download the Binance Wallet from the Chrome Store.



  2. Launch the Binance Wallet and select Use existing wallet.



  3. On your Ledger device, use the buttons to open the Binance Chain app. The display should read: Binance Chain ready. Now, select Connect Hardware Wallet.



  4. Choose BNB Beacon Chain Network, then click on Connect.





  5. In the new tab, click Grant access, choose your Ledger device, and then click Connect. If successful, the word Connected will appear on the screen.





  6. Re-open the Binance Wallet again and click Use existing wallet.



  7. Select Connect Hardware Wallet > BNB Beacon Chain Network and then choose Connect.
  8. From the provided list, select an account and click Confirm.



  9. Choose and confirm a password, agree to the terms of use by ticking the checkbox, and then click Confirm.



  10. Your Binance Beacon Chain account will now appear. Click the copy or Receive button to generate and verify your account address on your Ledger device. This address can be used to send BNB or BEP20 funds via the Binance Beacon Chain (BEP2) network.
