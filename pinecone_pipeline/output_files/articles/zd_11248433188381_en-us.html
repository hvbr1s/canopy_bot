How to hide a token in Ledger Live

To hide or unhide tokens in Ledger Live, please follow these steps.

Hiding a token

  Ledger Live Mobile   Ledger Live Desktop
  1. Open Ledger Live mobile.
  2. Make sure you are on the Wallet tab.
  3. Select the account holding the token you want to hide.
  4. Select the token you wish to hide.
  5. Click on the three little dots in the top right-hand corner of the app window.

  6. Select Hide Token.

  1. Open Ledger Live desktop.
  2. Click on the Accounts tab on the left side of the app. The page with all your accounts will open.

  3. Open Ledger Live, navigate to the Accounts section, and locate the specific token you wish to hide.
  4. Right-click on the token.



  5. Confirm by clicking Hide token.

Unhiding a token

  Ledger Live Mobile   Ledger Live Desktop
  1. Open Ledger Live mobile.
  2. Go to the Wallet tab.
  3. On the top right-hand corner click the Settings gear icon.



  4. Go to the Accounts section.
  5. Click on the "X" button associated with the token.



  1. Open Ledger Live desktop.
  2. Click on the Accounts tab on the left side of the app. The page with all your accounts will open.



  3. In Ledger Live, go to Settings >Accounts and navigate to the Hidden tokens section.
  4. Choose the token you want to unhide and click on the "X" button associated with the token.

If you're still having issues hiding or unhiding your tokens in Ledger Live, please don't hesitate to reach out to us. We're happy to help.
