Connect your Ledger device to Hiro Wallet

This tutorial describes how to connect Ledger Device to Hiro Wallet.

Before you start

  • Make sure the latest version of the Stacks (STX) app is installed on your Ledger device.
  • Make sure to close Ledger Live as it can conflict with the Hiro Wallet connection.

Connect your Ledger device to Hiro Wallet

  1. Navigate to Hiro wallet desktop app.
  2. Select your operating system MacOS/Windows/Linux in the top left corner and install Hiro Wallet.
  3. Open Hiro Wallet and select I already have a wallet.
  4. Select Continue with Ledger.
    Reminder: It is crucial to ensure the security of your 24-word recovery phrase (secret key). Never input it into a computer or smartphone, and refrain from sharing it with anyone.

  5. When prompted:
    • Connect your Ledger.
    • Open Stacks app on the Ledger device.
    • Select Continue on the Hiro wallet screen.
    • Confirm your address on your Ledger device by pressing both buttons.

Now you can send, receive, and view your Stacks balance.

How to view your Stacks balance

  1. Open the Hiro Wallet.
  2. Select I already have a wallet.
  3. Select Continue with Ledger.
  4. Connect your Ledger and enter PIN on the device.
  5. Open the Stacks app on your Ledger device.
  6. Select Continue to view the receive address on your Ledger device.
  7. Select Approve on your Ledger device.

Your total balance is shown at the top of the window under Total balance.

How to receive Stacks

  1. Open the Hiro Wallet.
  2. Select I already have a wallet.
  3. Select Continue with Ledger.
  4. Connect your Ledger and enter PIN on the device.
  5. Open the Stacks app on your Ledger device.
  6. Select Continue to view the receive address on your Ledger device.
  7. Select Approve on your Ledger device to proceed with using the Hiro Wallet.
  8. Select Receive at the top of the window under your total balance.
  9. Select Request Ledger address.
  10. Verify that the address shown in the Hiro Wallet matches the one shown on your Ledger device and select Approve.
  11. Copy the address shown in the Hiro Wallet to receive Stacks from it.

How to send Stacks

  1. Open the Hiro Wallet.
  2. Select I already have a wallet.
  3. Select Continue with Ledger.
  4. Connect your Ledger and enter PIN on the device.
  5. Open the Stacks app on your Ledger device.
  6. Select Continue to view the receive address on your Ledger device.
  7. Select Approve on your Ledger device.
  8. Select Send at the top of the window under your total balance.
  9. Enter the address to send STX, the amount of STX, and an optional memo describing the transfer.
  10. Select Preview then Sign transaction and send to initiate the transaction.
  11. Select Sign transaction and verify the address and all other details displayed on your Ledger device.
  12. Select Approve on your Ledger device to confirm and broadcast the transaction.
