"Invalid size for accountID" on Ethereum accounts in Ledger Live

This article explains the Invalid size for accountID error which may appear for some users in Ledger Live.

"Invalid size for accountID" error

You may encounter this error when trying to synchronize your Ethereum accounts with the blockchain. Our engineering team is actively investigating the issue. 

Temporary workaround

Zerion

If you are facing this issue, use Zerion in Ledger Live to check your problematic Ethereum balance.

Instructions on Zerion are available in this article.

MetaMask

To manage your problematic Ethereum balance, use your Ledger device with a third-party wallet MetaMask. 

Detailed instruction is available in this article.
