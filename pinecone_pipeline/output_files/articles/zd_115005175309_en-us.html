PoSW coin (POSW)

  Delisting of PoSW

  • POSW service on Ledger Live is terminated from February 28th, 2019.
  • Transactions and adding accounts will not be possible after the termination.
  • Uninstalling the PoSW app from your device in My Ledger remains possible.

Install the PoSW Coin app on your Ledger device to manage POSW with Ledger Live. The POSW app is developed and supported by Ledger and works on the Ledger Nano S and Ledger Blue devices.

Before you start

  • Set up Ledger Live with your device.
  • Update the firmware on your Ledger device.
  • Install the Bitcoin app, as POSW requires it.

Instructions

Install the POSW app
  1. Open My Ledger in Ledger Live.
  2. Connect and unlock your device.
  3. If asked, allow My Ledger to access your device.
  4. Find POSW in the app catalog.
  5. Click the Install button of the app.
    • An installation window appears.
    • Your device will display Processing...
    • The app installation is confirmed.
Configure public key exports

The POSW app can export your extended public key (xpub) to web apps in a U2F compatible web browser (Chrome, Opera or Firefox). The xpub is privacy-sensitive information, as all public addresses can be generated from it.

Public key exports by default require manual approval on the device to prevent inadvertent sharing of your public addresses. This setting can be configured as follows:

  1. Open the POSW app.
  2. Navigate to Settings > Public key export approval.
  3. Choose one of two options:
    • Manual approval (default): public key exports require a confirmation on the device.
    • Auto approval: public key exports are automatically approved.
POSW address formats

POSW addresses start with a D. This is the only address format.

  Address privacy

POSW transactions are public information. Do not re-use your addresses for optimal privacy. Ledger Live automatically generates new addresses and keeps track of your previous ones. Previous addresses do remain valid, but they don't offer an optimal level of privacy.
