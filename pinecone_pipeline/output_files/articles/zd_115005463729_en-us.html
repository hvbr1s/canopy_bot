OTG Kit: adapters for your Ledger devices

The Ledger OTG kit contains 3 different cables to connect your Ledger devices with as many compatible smartphones, tablets and computers as possible.

Purchase your Ledger OTG kit directly from Ledger or through the authorized distributor / reseller network to make sure you receive an authentic Ledger product.

Ledger devices run on

  • 64-bit Windows (8.1+)
  • 64-bit Mac OS (10.8+)
  • 64-bit Linux

Micro USB to USB Type-A cable

With this adapter you can connect:

  • a Nano to a smartphone / tablet
  • a HW.1 to a smartphone / tablet
  • a Nano S/X (with its own cable + this adapter) to a smartphone / tablet
  • a Blue (with its own cable + this adapter) to a smartphone / tablet

USB Type-C to USB Type-C cable

With this adapter you can connect:

  • a Nano S/X to a smartphone / tablet
  • a Blue to a smartphone / tablet

Micro USB to USB Type-C cable

With this adapter you can connect:

  • a Nano S/X to a smartphone / tablet / computer
  • a Blue to a smartphone / tablet / computer
