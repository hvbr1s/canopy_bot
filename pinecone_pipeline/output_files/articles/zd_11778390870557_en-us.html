Aleph Zero (AZERO)

This tutorial will show you how to create your first Aleph Zero (AZERO) account with your Ledger device and the Azero wallet

Before you start

  • Update Ledger Live through the notification banner or download the latest version.
  • Update the firmware on your Ledger Nano S Plus and Nano X.
  • We strongly recommend using Google Chrome for the best experience.

All set? Great, let’s dive in.

Install the Aleph Zero (AZERO) app on your Ledger device

  1. Connect and unlock your Ledger device.
  2. Open Ledger Live and navigate to My Ledger.
  3. If asked, allow access to My Ledger by pressing both buttons on your Ledger device simultaneously. 
  4. In My Ledger, select the App catalog, search for Aleph Zero (AZERO) and click Install.
    You're done.



     The Aleph Zero app is developed and supported by the Aleph Zero community.

Create your Aleph Zero (AZERO) account via Azero Wallet

  1. Go to https://azero.dev/
  2. Open the Aleph Zero app on your Ledger. Your device should display the message: Aleph Zero ready.



  3. Go to Settings > manage hardware connections, choose Attach Ledger via WebHID, and click Save.



  4. Next, navigate to the Accounts tab and click From Ledger. Name your account and click Save.



  5. From the menu, select your Ledger device and click Connect.



  6. Choose your newly added account and click the Copy button in the side panel to copy your account address.



    All set! You're ready to use this address to send AZERO coins to your Ledger wallet and manage your account.
