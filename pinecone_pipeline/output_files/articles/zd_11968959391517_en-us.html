Ledger OTC Desk by Coinify

This article explains how to access OTC (Over-the-Counter) service for purchasing $54000 or more in crypto in Ledger Live.

Accessing OTC provider Coinify+

  1. Open Ledger Live.
  2. Select the Buy/Sell tab in Ledger Live.

    • Buy: Enter the amount of crypto you want to Buy. The minimum is USD 54000 or 50000 Euro as of 03/07/2023.

    • Sell: Enter the coin and account you want to Sell from. If you select BTC, the minimum amount is 1.5 BTC as of 03/07/2023.

  4. Fill in all the information you requested in the form (your contact details, amount to purchase or sell).
  5. Once you submit the information, a personal assistant will be in touch with you shortly to help you with the operation.
