How to recover ETH sent to a CELO address

This article explains how to recover ETH sent to a CELO account address.

Before you start

  • Ensure you have the latest firmware version of the Ledger device.
  • Ensure the latest version of the Eth recovery app is installed on your Ledger device.
  • Once the app has been installed on your device, make sure to close Ledger Live.

Accessing your ETH via MyCrypto

  1. Open Ledger Live, go to Settings -> Experimental Features -> Enable Developer mode. 
  2. Go to My Ledger in Ledger Live. 
  3. Search for Eth recovery in the app catalog, and install the app on your Nano device.
  4. Open the Eth Recovery app on your Nano device.

  5. Close Ledger Live desktop application.
  6. Go to the MyCrypto - Add Account page.
  7. Select Ledger as the wallet option.
  8. Select Ethereum as the network and then click Next.
  9. Select Connect to Ledger wallet.
  10. Click the option add a custom derivation path on the top right of the window.

  11. In the Add Customer Derivation Path page, complete the following fields:
    • Label for New Derivation Path: ETH Celo
    • New Derivation Path Credentials: m/44'/52752'/0'/0
  12. Click on the Add DPath button. 

  13. In the drop-down list, select the ETH Celo derivation path you just created.

     After you have added this derivation path, more than one address may show up as selected. Unselect all accounts.

  14. Select your ETH Celo account, then click Add 1 account.

Your ETH (sent to CELO derived address) should show up on the MyCrypto.com home page. You can now select Send Assets on the left-hand side of the page, and send your “Celo” ETH out to your Ledger Live ETH account address.
