How to use your Ledger device with the Binance Wallet browser extension on the Binance Beacon Chain

This tutorial describes how to connect your Ledger device to the Binance Wallet browser extension on the Binance Beacon Chain.

Before you start

  • Ensure the latest version of the Binance Chain app is installed on your Ledger device.
    Â Note the Binance Chain app is different from the Binance Smart Chain app.

Connect your Ledger device to Binance Wallet

  1. Navigate to the Binance Wallet Chrome Web Store and click Add to Chrome.
  2. Open the Binance Wallet Extension and select Use existing wallet.
  3. Select Connect Hardware Wallet.
  4. Select BNB Beacon Chain Network.
  5. Open the Binance Chain app on your Ledger device.
  6. Click Connect.
  7. Click Grant access.
  8. Select your Ledger device from the pop-up that appears.
  9. Select the Ledger account(s) you want to view from the list and click Confirm.
  10. Set a wallet password and select Continue.

Your Binance Beacon Chain account appears.
