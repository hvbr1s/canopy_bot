Access Ledger Zcash account with third-party wallet YWallet

This article explains how to access a Ledger Zcash account using a third-party wallet YWallet if you are facing errors such as: 

  • "Internal error 0x6f01"
  • "-26:16: bad-txns-coinbase-spend-has-transparent-outputs"
  • "getVarint called with unexpected parameters"

Before you start

  • Make sure you tried to troubleshoot the Zcash errors by following these steps.

 For advanced users only

This tutorial involves entering your 24-word recovery phrase into a different third-party wallet YWallet. This is not something to be taken lightly, as anyone with your 24-word recovery phrase can control all accounts derived from it. Entering it into a computer is one of the least safe things you can do with your seed phrase. It can render it vulnerable to hacks, malware, and lots of vectors of attack that were previously impossible before taking this course of action. As such, please do it at your own risk.

Step-by-step

1- Sending your other assets to temporary addresses

  1. Send all your crypto assets (except the problematic Zcash account) to existing or temporary safe accounts. It can be accounts created on an exchange service, a software wallet, or any account linked to another hardware wallet.
  2. Remove the empty accounts in Ledger Live to avoid further confusion.

2- Installing YWallet

Once you have confirmed that all of your funds (except the problematic ZCash account) are now on other addresses and that the only asset left linked to your Ledger device is ZEC, go to the website of the Ywallet and download the version suited for your OS.

  This method works also with the mobile version of this wallet.

3- Accessing Ledger Zcash account in YWallet

  1. Open YWallet on your PC.
    The welcome screen appears.
  2. Click on New Account.
  3. Fill in the account name.
  4. Select Zcash and tick the box next to Restore an account.
  5. Enter your 24-word recovery phrase in the field.
    Make sure to enter all the words in the correct order as written in the recovery sheet.
  6. Click Import.
  7. Wait until the synchronization with the blockchain is complete. Depending on your internet speed and hardware, it can take from several minutes up to several hours.

Mobile: mobile version of this wallet will also take time to synchronize with the blockchain. As such, please make sure that your smartphone is charging or has an almost full battery while doing so. Additionally, if the smartphone enters sleep mode during this synchronization, it may end the process and will have to be started anew.

4- Managing ZEC in YWallet

Once the synchronization in YWallet is over, you’ll have access to the dashboard and other features of the wallet.

 Ledger Live displays transparent addresses, a feature of the Zcash network. The YWallet usually displays shielded addresses, you’ll have to click or tap on the QR Code to reveal your transparent address and balance!

  1. Click on the Send button.
  2. Insert the temporary address where you are sending your ZEC.
  3. Click on the “MAX” button to send your entire balance.
  4. Click Send.
  5. Double-check the transaction details and confirm again.

5 - Setting up a new 24-word recovery phrase on your Ledger device.

At this point, you can now reinitialize your Ledger device with a new 24-word recovery phrase and with new accounts.

  1. Enter 3 times incorrect PIN to reset Ledger device.
  2. Set up Ledger device as new.
  3. Install all the apps back (except Zcash).
  4. Add new accounts (except Zcash).
  5. Send your assets from temporary addresses back to Ledger Live.
