How to connect to Ledger Tezos account using Galleon Wallet

This article explains how to connect to the third-party wallet Galleon to access and manage a Ledger Tezos account.

Before we start

  • Make sure you have the latest firmware on your Ledger device.
  • Make sure you have the latest Tezos app installed on your Ledger device.
  • Make sure to close Ledger Live.

Connecting to Galleon Wallet

  1. Go to the Galleon Website.
  2. Download Galleon Wallet for your OS (Windows / macOS / Linux).
  3. Once installed, open Galleon Wallet.
  4. Select a language.
  5. Check and agree with the terms of service.Â 
  6. Click Get Started.
  7. Go to Settings on the top-right.
  8. Scroll down to Hardware Wallet Settings.
  9. Select Ledger Live derivation path and go back.
  10. Connect your Ledger device to your PC, unlock it, and open the Tezos app.
  11. Select Connect with Ledger.
  12. Review the request on your Ledger device and accept.

You should see your Ledger Tezos account balance now. You can now send/receive XTZ, stake, and manage your NFTs using your Ledger device and Galleon Wallet.

Check Galleon Tutorials to learn more on how to use the application.
