How to connect to MyNearWallet with your Ledger device

This article explains how to connect your Ledger device to the NEAR network via MyNearWallet.

Before you start

  • Download and install the latest version of Ledger Live. 
  • Update the firmware on your Ledger Nano S / Nano S Plus / Nano X.

All set? Great, let’s dive in.

Install the NEAR app on your Ledger device

  1. Connect your Ledger device and unlock it.
  2. In the Ledger Live app, navigate to My Ledger.
  3. Confirm that you allow My Ledger to access your device. You can do that by clicking both buttons on your device simultaneously. 
  4. Go back to Ledger Live. In My Ledger, head over to App catalog and search for NEAR.
  5. Click Install.

Connect your Ledger device to MyNearWallet

  1. Navigate to MyNearWallet.
  2. Select Import Account.
  3. Click Sign in with Ledger.
  4. Open NEAR app on your Ledger device.
  5. Click on Advanced options button.
    • If you have only one NEAR account in Ledger Live, select HD path as 0.
    • If you have more than one NEAR account in Ledger Live, do the following:
      • 1st account HD path should be 0.
      • 2nd NEAR account HD path should be 1.
      • 3rd NEAR account HD path should be 2.

        Please note:         Creating a second NEAR account in Ledger Live? You may face difficulty locating this account in NEAR web wallets and various third-party NEAR wallet applications.
  6. Click Set HD Path and Authorize connection. A connection window pops up.
  7. Click Connect.
    • If a connection window pops up, select Ledger device and Connect.
  8. Approve the public key on your Ledger device.

Attention Ledger Near Users

When setting up MyNearWallet with your Ledger device for the first time, take note that the "Set HD Path" defaults to "1." However, please ensure you set the index to "0" (44'/397'/0'/0'/0) during the setup process. This ensures alignment with Ledger Live application's default derivation path for the first NEAR account on Ledger Hardware wallets.

Now, you should be able to see MyNearWallet Dashboard and manage your Ledger NEAR account in MyNearWallet with your Ledger device.

Check MyNearWallet Help Center to learn about all the features of the wallet.
