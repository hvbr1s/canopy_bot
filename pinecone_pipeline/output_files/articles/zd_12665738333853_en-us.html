Kaspa (KAS)

This article provides updated information about Kaspa (KAS) which is currently not supported by Ledger.
 You can learn more about the Kaspa project here.

What's Kaspa? 

Kaspa claims to be the "fastest, open-source, decentralized & fully scalable Layer-1 in the world" and "the world’s first blockDAG – a digital ledger enabling parallel blocks and instant transaction confirmation – built on a robust proof-of-work engine with rapid single-second block intervals."

You can learn more about the technology behind Kaspa technical documentation.

Does Ledger support Kaspa (KAS)? 

No, Ledger does not provide support for Kaspa (KAS) yet but we're working with the Kaspa team to bring a Kaspa app to your Ledger device as soon as possible.

Updates will be posted here and on the Ledger Support Twitter account.

What can I expect from future integrations with Ledger?

Once the Kaspa app becomes available to download in Ledger Live, you'll be able to install the app on your Ledger device and create a Ledger Kaspa account via third-party apps to store your KAS.

What should I do for now?

Hang tight! You can follow us on Twitter for updates. Learn more about how to manage crypto assets with your Ledger device here.
