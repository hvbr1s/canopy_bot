403 Error - The request could not be satisfied. Request blocked

This article describes the '403 Error - The request could not be satisfied. Request blocked' that you may encounter when trying to download the Ledger Live app from the Ledger website at ledger.com/ledger-live

This error can happen for one of the following reasons: 

  • You're using a work computer or computer with restricted connection.
  • Your internet service provider is blocking or interfering with the Ledger website.

Troubleshoot

  • Attempt the download using a personal computer without connection restrictions.
  • Reach out to your internet service provider regarding potential access restrictions.

Still stuck?
