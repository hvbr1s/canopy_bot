How to connect your Ledger device to Compound finance

This tutorial describes how to connect your Ledger device and accounts to Compound Finance.

Before you start

  • Ensure the latest version of the Ethereum (ETH) app is installed on your Ledger device.
  • Ensure blind signing is enabled on the ETH app.

Connect your Ledger device to Compound Finance

  1. Navigate to Compound Finance.
  2. Open the ETH app on your device.
  3. Click Connect Wallet.
  4. Select Ledger.
  5. Keep the default option Ledger Live and click Select Address.
    A drop-down menu will appear.
  6. Select an account to connect to Compound.

Your Ledger device and selected account are now connected to Compound Finance.

Disconnect your Ledger device from Compound Finance

  1. Click the connected account.
  2. Select Disconnect Wallet.

Your Ledger device and account are now disconnected from Compound.
