How to re-bag your Polkadot (DOT) stake

This tutorial describes how to manage bags lists with polkadot.js.

What are bags lists? 

Introduced in March 2022, the "bags list" system sorts nominating accounts into "bags" based on the amount of DOT staked. Only the top 22,500 accounts are factored into the reward algorithm, making the minimum staked amount required for rewards* a dynamic figure (equivalent to 502 DOT coins as of November, 2023). If your DOT stake surpasses this minimum amount but you're not seeing rewards, it's possible that your account is not optimally positioned within its bag. As a result, your account may be inadvertently bypassed during the reward process. This is especially the case if you've recently added DOT coins to your bonded stake. You can learn more about bags list here.

Before you start

  • Download and install Ledger Live on your computer. You can learn more about how to get started with Ledger Live here.
  • Open Ledger Live, connect your Ledger device to My Ledger and make sure your device is running the latest firmware version.
  • Update the firmware of your Ledger Nano S / Nano S Plus / Nano X.

Install the Polkadot (DOT) app on your Ledger device

  1. Open Ledger Live and navigate to the My Ledger  tab.
  2. Connect and unlock your Ledger device.
  3. If prompted, allow My Ledger on your device.
  4. Search for the Polkadot app in the app catalog.
  5. Click the Install button of the app.
    After a short moment, the word Installed appears in Ledger Live, confirming the installation is successful.

Re-bag your DOT stake using your Ledger and polkadot.js

  1. Go to polkadot.js.org/apps
  2. Follow these steps to connect your Ledger device to polkadot.js.
  3. Once your Ledger is connected, go to Network > Staking and select the Bags tab.



  4. Click on Move up then review and confirm the transaction on your Ledger device.



    You've successfully completed the process. Please note, however, that after re-bagging your stake, it may take a few days for DOT rewards to be reflected in Ledger Live.

I am not able to see the Move up option

It may take up to 20 minutes for the Move up option to appear in the interface. However, you can potentially expedite this by switching to a different connecting node.

To do so, click on the Polygon logo in the top left corner, choose a different node, and confirm your selection by pressing the Switch button. Additionally, the Move up option might not show up if you've recently re-bagged your stake.
