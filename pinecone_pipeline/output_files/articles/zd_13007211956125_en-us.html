Fixing Cardano Transaction Broadcast Issues

We are aware some users face issues when managing their Cardano (ADA) account(s) in Ledger Live.

Actions such as sending, delegating (staking), or ending a delegation (unstaking) ADA may result in an error message, or the transaction never being broadcasted. This article provides steps to address the issue and offers an alternative solution if the issue persists.

Step one: Clear the cache in Ledger Live

  1. Go to Settings in Ledger Live by clicking on the gear icon on the upper right-hand side.
  2. Go to the Help tab.
  3. Click on the Clear Cache button.
  4. Click on Confirm in the window that appears. 

 

Step Two: Re-add your Cardano (ADA) account

  1. Navigate to your Cardano account settings and click Remove from Portfolio.



  2. Click Confirm.



  3. Navigate to the Accounts section and re-add your Cardano (ADA) account in Ledger live with your Ledger device.



    You're all done! Once your account is re-added to Ledger Live, you should be able to continue using it as usual.

The issue persists

If the issue persists after clearing the cache and re-adding your Cardano (ADA) account, we recommend using a third-party app like AdaLite to manage your ADA transactions. Learn more about how to connect to AdaLite here.

If you have concerns about connecting your Ledger Nano to a third party wallet, rest assured that this does not compromise the security of your assets. Learn more here.
