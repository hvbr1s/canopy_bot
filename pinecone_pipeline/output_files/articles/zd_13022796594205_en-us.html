Obsolete Applications in Ledger Live

This article provides information about the removal of certain obsolete applications from the Ledger Live catalog. While these applications are removed from the catalog, you can still uninstall them from your Ledger devices via the "My Ledger" tab in Ledger Live. This action is being taken due to various reasons such as discontinued projects, lack of activity, or migration to other platforms.

Impact on Ledger Live Users

After the removal of these applications from the Ledger Live catalog, you will no longer be able to install or manage these applications directly from Ledger Live. However, if you've previously installed any of these applications on their Ledger devices, you will still have the option to uninstall them.

What should you do?

If you currently have any of the aforementioned applications installed on your Ledger device and you no longer wish to use them, you can follow these steps to uninstall them:

  1. Click on the My Ledger tab in the left panel.
  2. Connect and unlock your Ledger device by entering the PIN code.
  3. Press both buttons to allow My Ledger (Manager access) on your device.
  4. Select the obsolete application you wish to uninstall from the list, then click to select it.
  5. Tap the Trash button (represented by the trash can icon) associated with the app.

 

Upon completion of the uninstallation process, the application will be removed from your Ledger device.

Additional assistance

If you encounter any difficulties during the uninstallation process or have further questions about removing these obsolete applications, please don't hesitate to contact our customer support team.
