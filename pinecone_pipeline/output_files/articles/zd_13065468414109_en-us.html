Pendulum (PEN)

This tutorial will show you how to create your first Ledger Pendulum (PEN) account with your Ledger device and polkadot.js.
 The Pendulum app is developed and supported by the Pendulum community.

Before you start

  • Update Ledger Live through the notification banner or download the latest version.
  • Update the firmware on your Ledger Nano S / Nano S Plus / Nano X.

All set? Great, let’s dive in.

Install the Pendulum (PEN) app on your Ledger device

  1. Connect and unlock your Ledger device.
  2. Open Ledger Live and navigate to My Ledger.
  3. If asked, allow access to My Ledger by pressing both buttons on your Ledger device simultaneously. 
  4. In My Ledger, select the App catalog, search for Pendulum (PEN) and click Install.
    You're done.
