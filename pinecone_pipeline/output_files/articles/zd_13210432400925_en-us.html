How to manage your crypto portfolio with Nested Finance through Ledger Live

Nested Finance is a DeFi trading platform that allows you to easily build, copy, manage, and monetize your DeFi portfolio.

You can access Nested through the Discover section of Ledger Live. All transactions will have to be approved by your Ledger device. 

Before you begin

  • Make sure that you’re running the latest version of Ledger Live and the latest OS version on your Ledger device. 
  • You will need an account for the crypto you want to manage with Nested → Learn more

How to access Nested through Ledger Live

  1. In the Ledger Live app, navigate to the left sidebar menu and click Discover.
  2. Search for and click Nested.

  1. Click Continue. You’ll land on the Explorer page where you can see the overview of the featured portfolios, most copied traders, most trusted and best-performing portfolios, and more.

Connect your portfolio to Nested

  1. Click My holdings in the left navigation bar. You’ll land on the Overview page.
  2. Click Connect to add your portfolio.

  1. Connect your Ledger device and enter the PIN to unlock it.
  2. Click Sign message.

  1. Review transaction details.
  2. Approve the transaction by pressing both buttons of your Ledger device.
