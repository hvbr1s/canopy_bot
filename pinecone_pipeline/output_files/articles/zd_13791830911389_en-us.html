How to create an Ethereum Goerli account

This article describes how to create an account on the Ethereum Goerli testnet with your Ledger device and Ledger Live.

 Goerli testnet is deprecated

Starting January 2023, Goerli testnet will be deprecated but will continue to receive support for an additional 12 months. We recommend using Sepolia for future activities.

Before you start

  • Download and install the latest version of Ledger Live via this link. 
  • Update the firmware on your Ledger Nano S, Nano S Plus, or Nano X.
  • Enable Developer Mode in Ledger Live by navigating to Settings > Experimental Features.

Install the Ethereum (ETH) app on your Ledger device

  1. Open Ledger Live and navigate to My Ledger tab.
  2. Connect and unlock your Ledger device.
  3. If asked, allow My Ledger on your device.
  4. Search for the Ethereum (ETH) app in the app catalog.
  5. Click the Install button to install the app on your Ledger device.
    • Your Ledger device displays Processing.
    • Ledger Live displays Installed.

Add an Ethereum Goerli account to Ledger Live

  1. Connect your Ledger device, unlock it with your PIN and open the Ethereum (ETH) app.
    Your Ledger device should display the words: Application is ready.
  2. In Ledger Live, navigate to the Accounts tab then click +Add account.
  3. Select Ethereum Goerli (ETH) and click Continue.



  4. Select an account by ticking the box and then click Add account.



  5. Click Done to finalize adding your Ethereum Goerli account to Ledger Live.
