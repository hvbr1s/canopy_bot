Global-e FAQ

After you have made a purchase on our official Ledger website, you might have noticed the mention “Sold by Global-e” on your receipt.

This article provides information on the role of Global-e, an e-commerce solution and what this partnership means for Ledger customers.

What is Global-e?
Global-e is a company that offers e-commerce solutions designed to simplify and optimize the online shopping experience. Ledger is using Global-e’s solution as part of its robust e-commerce offering to sell Ledger products on our official Ledger website as of October 9th, 2023.
Why is Ledger using Global-e?

Ledger has partnered with Global-e to enhance your shopping experience on Ledger.com. Thanks to Global-e, Ledger now offers the convenience of purchasing Ledger products in your local currency. During the checkout process, you’ll have the option to pay the total amount, which includes any applicable sales taxes (such as VAT), as well as international delivery costs and fees. If they are available for pre-payment, this price also covers any import duties, tariffs, or similar fees that may be required by your delivery destination country.

 Important information about custom fees

According to Global-e Sales Terms and Conditions, custom fees cannot be refunded when you request a product refund.

Are there any changes for Ledger customers?
Ledger continues to manage Ledger products manufacturing, packaging, product handling, logistics, customer support, and after-sales services, among other things. Things to know about the partnership between Ledger and Global-e:
  • Global-e as the seller on Ledger.com 
    Global-e acts as the seller for your purchases on Ledger.com. As the seller, ‘Global-e' is your co-contractor and therefore, the name you’ll see on your bank statement when you make a purchase on Ledger.com. As mentioned above, this does not change anything around the manufacturing or shipping of your product, and therefore the security of your Ledger.

  • Personal data
