How to change the date format in Ledger Live

This tutorial guides you through changing the date/time format on both Ledger Live desktop and mobile

Change the date format on Ledger Live

  Ledger Live Desktop   Ledger Live Mobile
  1. Open the Ledger Live app on your laptop or desktop computer.
  2. Click on the settings gear  , then select General.
  3. In the Region section, select your region from the drop-down menu. This action will change the date format to match that of the selected region.
  4. Finally restart Ledger Live for the change to take effect.

  1. Open the Ledger Live app on your phone.
  2. Click on the settings gear  , then select General.
  3. In the Date Format section, select DD/MM/YYYY or MM/DD/YYY.
  4. Finally restart Ledger Live for the change to take effect.
