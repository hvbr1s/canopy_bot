MobileCoin (MOB)

This guide provides step-by-step instructions on how to set up your first MobileCoin (MOB) account using a Ledger device.

Please note, Ledger Live does not currently support MobileCoin accounts. To manage your MobileCoin MOB coins, you will need to connect your Ledger device to the desktop version of the MobileCoin Wallet app.

At this time, the MobileCoin feature is exclusively available for Nano S Plus users. The capability for Nano X is in active development. Before you start

  • Download and install the latest version of Ledger Live via this link.
  • Make sure your Ledger device runs the latest firmware. Learn more.

Install the MobileCoin app on your Ledger device

  1. Open Ledger Live.
  2. Go to My Ledger.
  3. Connect and unlock your Ledger device.
  4. If asked, allow My Ledger on your Ledger device.
  5. Find MobileCoin in the app catalog.
  6. Click the Install button of the app.
    After a moment the MobileCoin app should appear on your Ledger device.
    You are now ready to use your Ledger device with MobileCoin!

Create a MobileCoin account with your Ledger device and MobileCoin Wallet

Mobile users
Currently, the MobileCoin Wallet app for mobile devices does not support Ledger devices. To create a MobileCoin account, please use your Ledger Nano S Plus with the MobileCoin desktop wallet, which is compatible with Linux and macOS systems. Windows support is still being developed by the MobileCoin team.
Desktop users
  1. First, ensure that Ledger Live is completely closed.

  2. Download the MobileCoin Wallet for desktop from the official site.

  3. Open the MobileCoin Wallet, choose a password and then start to review the TERMS OF USE.



  4. Check the box indicating you've read and agree to the terms, then select Create Wallet.

  5. Connect your Ledger Nano S Plus to your desktop computer.
  6. Open the MobileCoin app on your Ledger device. You should see 'MobileCoin is ready' displayed on the screen.
  7. On the desktop Wallet, choose a username and then select 'Import Account Using Ledger'.



  8. Follow the prompts on your Ledger device to navigate to the 'Approve Sync' screen, and press both buttons to confirm synchronization with your wallet.
  9. Set and confirm a PIN for MobileCoin Wallet and then click 'Set Pin' to finalize it.

  10. To receive funds, navigate to the 'Send/Receive' section and click the 'Receive' tab.
    Your public address will be displayed here; this is the address you'll use to receive MobileCoin MOB coins into your Ledger account.
