API HTTP 503 error on Ethereum (ETH) accounts

Ethereum (ETH) users may encounter 'API HTTP 503' errors while managing their accounts or when sending and receiving ETH and ERC20 tokens.

This error indicates that the Ledger Live servers are experiencing an issue or are currently overloaded and cannot process your transaction. This is often due to high traffic on the Ethereum network.

Troubleshooting

  • Try your transaction again later. You can monitor the activity on the Ethereum network at: https://etherscan.io/gastracker
  • Consider using an alternative to Ledger Live, such as Metamask or Rabby until the issue is fixed.
