Temporary Error (November 2023): "Txn-mempool-conflict" When Sending BTC

Issues affecting Bitcoin (BTC) Send and Swap from Ledger Live

  • We are aware of errors affecting some Ledger Live instances' ability to broadcast BTC transactions. 
  • When attempting to send BTC or perform a swap from BTC, you may receive the error txn-mempool-conflict and be unable to broadcast any transactions.
  • We're actively investigating the root cause to restore normal service as soon as possible.
  • Your funds are safe.

Temporary solution to manage your Bitcoin (BTC)

1. Clear your cache by opening the Ledger Live Settings > Help > Clear Cache.

2. If that doesn't help, you can safely use your Ledger device with Electrum to access and manage your Bitcoin (BTC) assets while waiting for the fix. Learn more about how to use Electrum here.

 

Once the fix will be deployed, we will notify users on our Ledger Support page on Twitter.
