How to Fix the "Invalid Time Value" Error in Ledger Live

If you're encountering the "Invalid Time Value" crash when using Ledger Live, don't worry - there's a straightforward fix.

Troubleshooting the error

  1. Reset Ledger Live: Begin the reset process for Ledger Live by navigating to:
    Settings > Help > and then Reset Ledger Live.

  2. Reconnect Your Device: After the reset, click on Connect followed by Device.
    If you already have a recovery phrase, select Already have a Recovery Phrase to ensure your accounts are properly linked to your device.

    After resetting Ledger Live, follow the prompts to re-link your device with the application.

  3. Add Accounts: Return to the main page and proceed through the Add Accounts process. This will allow you to restore visibility and management of your crypto assets within Ledger Live.

By following these steps, you should resolve the "Invalid Time Value" error and restore full functionality to your Ledger Live application.
