Firo (ex-Zcoin)

Zcoin becomes FIRO

  • In October 2020, the Zcoin project was rebranded to Firo. Learn more here.
  • You can learn more about the project's new mission and roadmap here.
  • More information on how to set up a Ledger Firo (FIRO) account is available below. 

This article will help you set up your first Ledger Firo (FIRO) account. Check the Firo wallet page to learn more.

 The FIRO app is developed and supported by the Firo community.

Before you start

  • Download and install Ledger Live on your computer. You can learn more about how to get started with Ledger Live here.
  • Update the firmware of your Ledger Nano S / Nano S Plus / Nano X.

Install the Firo (FIRO) app on your Ledger device

  1. Open Ledger Live and navigate to the My Ledger tab.
  2. Connect and unlock your Ledger device.
  3. If asked, allow My Ledger to access your device.
  4. Search for the Firo (FIRO) app in the app catalog.
  5. Click the Install button to install the app on your Ledger device.
    • Your Ledger device displays Processing.
    • Ledger Live displays Installed.

Final Steps

  • Ledger Live does not support Firo accounts for the moment.
  • To create your first Ledger Firo (FIRO) account or access an existing Firo account, please follow the steps below:
  1. Download the Electrum wallet from this link
  2. Connect your Ledger device to Electrum by following these steps.
  3. Need help? Please reach out to the Firo community. 

Advanced features

Configure public key exports

The Firo app can export your extended public key (xpub) to web apps in a U2F compatible web browser (Chrome, Opera, or Firefox). The xpub is privacy-sensitive information, as all public addresses can be generated from it.

Public key exports require by default manual approval on the device to prevent inadvertent sharing of your public addresses. This setting can be configured as follows:

  1. Open the Firo app.
  2. Navigate to Settings > Public key export approval.
  3. Choose one of two options:
    • Manual approval (default): public key exports require a confirmation on the device.
    • Auto approval: public key exports are automatically approved.
