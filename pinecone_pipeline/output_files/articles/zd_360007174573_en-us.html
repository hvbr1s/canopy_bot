Export logs

The Ledger Live application saves a detailed timeline of its events in a log file. Exporting these logs may be useful for troubleshooting purposes.

Use keyboard shortcut in Ledger Live Desktop

  1. Press CTRL + E on your keyboard immediately after encountering a bug or crash.
  2. Choose where to save the logs on your computer.
  3. Click Save. 
  4. Attach the saved logs (JSON file) to your customer request.

Export from Ledger Live settings

  Ledger Live Desktop   Ledger Live Mobile
  1. Click on Settings cog in the top right corner of the app.
  2. Click on the Help tab.
  3. Navigate to the Save logs section and click Save.
  4. Choose where to save the logs on your computer.
  5. Click Save.
  6. Attach the saved logs (JSON file) to your customer request.
  1. Tap on the  Settings icon on the Portfolio tab.
  2. Tap on the Help section.
  3. Choose Save Logs.
  4. Choose where to save the logs on your phone.
  5. Attach the saved logs (JSON file) to your customer request.

Export from an error message

  Ledger Live Desktop   Ledger Live Mobile

You can export the logs directly from an error message in Ledger Live Desktop.

  1. Once you see an error message in Ledger Live, click Save logs.
  2. Choose where to save the logs on your computer.
  3. Click Save.
  4. Attach the saved logs (JSON file) to your customer request.

You can export the logs directly from an error message in Ledger Live Mobile.

  1. Once you see an error message in Ledger Live Mobile, click Save logs.
  2. Choose where to save the logs on your phone.
  3. Attach the saved logs (JSON file) to your customer request.
