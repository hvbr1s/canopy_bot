(Ledger) Chrome apps deactivated

The Chrome apps have been replaced by Ledger Live, which provides a single app to manage your Ledger device and the crypto assets it secures.

 Beware of fake Ledger apps

Ledger users are targeted by fake Ledger apps, also advertised via search engines. Don't fall for it! Any application tricking you into entering your recovery phrase will steal your crypto assets.     

  • Verify that you download Ledger Live only from ledger.com/ledger-live/download.
  • Never enter your recovery phrase anywhere else than on your Ledger device. If an app asks for your recovery phrase, it is a malicious app and not from Ledger. Learn more 

No longer supported

Ledger Chrome apps have been deactivated and replaced by Ledger Live. The following applications are no longer supported: 

  • Ledger Manager
  • Ledger Bitcoin Wallet app
  • Ledger Ethereum Chrome app 
  • Ledger Ripple Wallet
