Ontology (ONT)

Install the Ontology app on your Ledger device to manage ONT and ONG tokens with the OWallet application. The Ontology app is developed and supported by the Ontology community. Check the Ontology wallet page to learn more.

  Ontology accounts can not be added to Ledger Live.

Before you start

  • Set up Ledger Live with your Ledger device.
  • Update the firmware of your Ledger Nano S / Nano S Plus / Nano X.

Install ONT app on Ledger device

  1. Open My Ledger in Ledger Live.
  2. Connect and unlock your Ledger device.
  3. If asked, allow My Ledger to access your device.
  4. Find ONT in the app catalog.
    Note: ONT was renamed from Ontology and available on firmware version 1.5.5 and higher.
  5. Click the Install button of the app.
    • An installation window appears.
    • Your device will display Processing... 
    • The app installation is confirmed.
