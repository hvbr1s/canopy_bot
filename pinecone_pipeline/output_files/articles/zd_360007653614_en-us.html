ICON (ICX)

Install the ICON app on your Ledger device to manage ICX tokens with the ICONex wallet. The ICON app is developed and supported by the ICON community. Check the ICON wallet page to learn more.

  ICON accounts cannot be added to Ledger Live.

Before you start

  • Set up Ledger Live with your Ledger device.
  • Update the firmware of your Ledger Nano S / Nano S Plus / Nano X.

Install the ICON app

  1. Open My Ledger in Ledger Live.
  2. Connect and unlock your Ledger device.
  3. If asked, allow My Ledger to access your device.
  4. Find ICON in the app catalog.
  5. Click the Install button of the app.
    • An installation window appears.
    • Your device will display Processing... 
    • The app installation is confirmed.

Next steps

  • Reach out to the ICON community for support.

Voting and staking not supported

Voting and staking are not officially supported by the ICON app. The information displayed for these features is always the same, meaning that the user will sign blindly.
