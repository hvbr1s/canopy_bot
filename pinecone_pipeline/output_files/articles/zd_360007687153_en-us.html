Particl (PART)

Install the Particl app on your device to manage PART tokens with the Particl Core Qt wallet.

  Particl accounts can not be added to Ledger Live. Learn more about how to manage crypto assets with your Ledger device here.

Before you start 

  • Set up Ledger Live with your Ledger device.
  • Update the firmware of your Ledger Nano S / Nano S Plus / Nano X.
  • Activate Developer mode in Ledger Live in Settings > Experimental features.

Install the Particl app

  1. Open My Ledger in Ledger Live.
  2. Connect and unlock your Ledger device.
  3. If asked, allow My Ledger to access your device.
  4. Find Particl in the app catalog.
  5. Click the Install button of the app.



    The Particl app is developed and supported by the Particl community.
