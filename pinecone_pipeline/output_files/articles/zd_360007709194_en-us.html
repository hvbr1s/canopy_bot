Solve error 0x6b00

This article explains how to solve the 0x6b00 error that usually happens when you try to sign a transaction with outdated firmware or the outdated Nano app. 

Your Ledger device may return the following error message:

Something went wrong. Please replug your device.
Ledger device: Invalid parameter received (0x6b00).

How to solve error 0x6b00?

To solve this error, try the following: 

  • Ensure that your device runs the latest firmware version.
  • Reinstall the apps on your device so you run the latest versions. Uninstalling apps does not affect your crypto assets—they are secured on the blockchain.

Are you still getting errors?

If you continue to see the 0x6b00 error, please contact our Support team.
