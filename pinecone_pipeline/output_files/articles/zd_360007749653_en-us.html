Kowala (kUSD)

  The kUSD network no longer available

The kUSD network is not available. You can no longer manage kUSD. However, it is still possible to uninstall the Kowala app. 

Install the Kowala app on your Ledger device to manage kUSD tokens with Kowala. The Kowala app is developed and supported by the Kowala community.

 Kowala accounts cannot be added to Ledger Live. Learn more about how to manage your crypto assets with your Ledger device here.

Before you start

  • Set up Ledger Live with your Ledger device.
  • Update the firmware on your Ledger hardware wallet.
  • Get Google Chrome or Firefox.
  • Install the Ethereum app, as Kowala requires it.

Install the Kowala app

  1. Open My Ledger in Ledger Live.
  2. Connect and unlock your Ledger device.
  3. If asked, allow My Ledger to access your device.
  4. Find Kowala in the app catalog.
  5. Click the Install button of the app.
    • An installation window appears.
    • Your device will display Processing... 
    • The app installation is confirmed.
