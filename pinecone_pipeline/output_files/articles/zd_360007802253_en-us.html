Proof of Authority (POA)

Install the Proof of Authority app on your Ledger device to manage POA with MyCrypto or MyEtherWallet. The POA app is developed and supported by the POA community.

 POA accounts can not be added to Ledger Live. Learn more about how to manage your crypto assets with your Ledger device here.

Before you start

  • Set up Ledger Live with your Ledger device.
  • Update the firmware of your Ledger Nano S / Nano S Plus / Nano X.
  • Install Firefox or Google Chrome.
  • The Ethereum app is installed on your device.

Install the POA app

  1. Open My Ledger in Ledger Live.
  2. Connect and unlock your Ledger device.
  3. If asked, allow My Ledger to access your device.
  4. Find POA in the app catalog.
  5. Click the Install button of the app.
    • An installation window appears.
    • Your device will display Processing... 
    • The app installation is confirmed.

Next steps

  • Check the MyCrypto or MyEtherWallet user guides for setup instructions.
  • Reach out to the POA community for support.
