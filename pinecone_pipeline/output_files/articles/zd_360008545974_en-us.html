Hycon (HYC)

 Upcoming removal from Ledger Live

The Hycon project is currently inactive. Consequently, the HYCON (HYC) app will soon be removed from Ledger Live and will no longer be available for download. If you're a HYCON user or affiliated with the Hycon team, please reach out to our support.

Install the Hycon app on your Ledger device to manage HYC with the Hycon Desktop Wallet. The Hycon app is developed and supported by the Hycon community.

 Hycon accounts cannot be added to Ledger Live. Learn more about how to manage your crypto assets with your Ledger device here.

Before you start

  • Set up Ledger Live with your Ledger device.
  • Update the firmware of your Ledger Nano S / Nano S Plus / Nano X.
  • Get Google Chrome or Firefox.

Install the Hycon app

  1. Open My Ledger in Ledger Live.
  2. Connect and unlock your Ledger device.
  3. If asked, allow My Ledger to access your device.
  4. Find Hycon in the app catalog.
  5. Click the Install button of the app.
    • An installation window appears.
    • Your device will display Processing... 
    • The app installation is confirmed.
