Pirl (PIRL)

Install the Pirl app on your Ledger device to manage PIRL with MyEtherWallet.

 Pirl accounts cannot be added to Ledger Live. Learn more about how to manage crypto assets with your Ledger device here.

Before you start

  • Set up Ledger Live with your Ledger device.
  • Update the firmware of your Ledger Nano S / Nano S Plus / Nano X.
  • Google Chrome or Firefox is installed.
  • Install the Ethereum app, as Pirl requires it.
  • Activate Developer mode in Ledger Live in Settings > Experimental features.

Install the Pirl app

  1. Open My Ledger in Ledger Live.
  2. Connect and unlock your Ledger device.
  3. If asked, allow My Ledger to access your device.
  4. Find Pirl in the app catalog.
  5. Click the Install button of the app.



    The Pirl app is developed and supported by the Pirl community.

Next steps

  • Check the MyEtherWallet user guide for setup instructions.
  • Reach out to the Pirl community on their forum or on Reddit if you need assistance.
