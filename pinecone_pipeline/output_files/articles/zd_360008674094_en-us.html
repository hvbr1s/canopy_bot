Akroma (AKA)

Install the Akroma app on your Ledger device to manage AKA with MyEtherWallet.

 Akroma accounts cannot be added to Ledger Live.

Before you start

  • Set up Ledger Live with your Ledger device.
  • Update the firmware of your Ledger Nano S / Nano S Plus / Nano X.
  • Get Google Chrome or Firefox.
  • Install the Ethereum app, as Akroma requires it.
  • Activate Developer mode in Ledger Live in Settings > Experimental features.

Install the Akroma (AKA) app

  1. Open My Ledger in Ledger Live.
  2. Connect and unlock your Ledger device.
  3. If asked, allow My Ledger to access your device.
  4. Find Akroma in the app catalog.
  5. Click the Install button of the app.



    The AKA app is developed and supported by the Akroma community.

Next steps

  • Check the MyEtherWallet user guide for setup instructions.
  • Reach out to the Akroma community on Reddit if you need assistance.
