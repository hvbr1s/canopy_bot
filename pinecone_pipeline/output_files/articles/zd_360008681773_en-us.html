Waves (WAVES)

Install the Waves app on your Ledger device to manage WAVES with the Waves.Exchange. The Waves app is developed and supported by the Waves community. Check the Waves wallet page to learn more.

 Waves accounts cannot be added to Ledger Live.

Before you start

  • Set up Ledger Live with your Ledger device.
  • Update the firmware of your Ledger Nano S / Nano S Plus / Nano X.
  • Install a U2F compatible browser such as Google Chrome.

Install the Waves app

  1. Open My Ledger in Ledger Live.
  2. Connect and unlock your Ledger device.
  3. If asked, allow My Ledger to access your device.
  4. Find Waves in the app catalog.
  5. Click the Install button of the app.
    • An installation window appears.
    • Your device will display Processing... 
    • The app installation is confirmed.
