FIC Network (eFIC)

 Upcoming removal from Ledger Live

The FIC Network blockchain is currently inactive and not producing blocks. Consequently, the FIC Network app will soon be removed from Ledger Live and will no longer be available for download. If you're a FIC user or affiliated with the FIC Network team, please reach out to our support.

Install the FIC app on your Ledger device to manage FIC coins with the FIC wallet application. The FIC app is developed and supported by the FIC Network.

 FIC accounts cannot be added to Ledger Live.

Before you start

  • Set up Ledger Live with your Ledger device.
  • Update the firmware of your Ledger Nano S / Nano S Plus / Nano X.

Install the FIC app

  1. Open My Ledger in Ledger Live.
  2. Connect and unlock your Ledger device.
  3. If asked, allow My Ledger to access your device.
  4. Find FIC in the app catalog.
  5. Click the Install button of the app.
    • An installation window appears.
    • Your device will display Processing...
    • The app installation is confirmed.

Next steps
