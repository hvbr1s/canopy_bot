EtherGem (EGEM)

Install the EtherGem app on your Ledger device to manage EGEM with MyCrypto or MyEtherWallet. The EGEM app is developed and supported by the EtherGem community.

 EGEM accounts cannot be added to Ledger Live. Learn more about how to manage your crypto assets with your Ledger device here.

Before you start

  • Set up Ledger Live with your Ledger device.
  • Update the firmware of your Ledger Nano S / Nano S Plus / Nano X.
  • Get Google Chrome or Firefox.
  • Install the Ethereum app, as EtherGem requires it.

Install the EGEM app

  1. Open My Ledger in Ledger Live.
  2. Connect and unlock your Ledger device.
  3. If asked, allow My Ledger to access your device.
  4. Find EtherGem in the app catalog.
  5. Click the Install button of the app.
    • An installation window appears.
    • Your device will display Processing... 
    • The app installation is confirmed.

Next steps

  • Check the MyCrypto or MyEtherWallet user guides for setup instructions.
  • Reach out to the EGEM community on Discord for support.
