GoChain (GO)

Install the GoChain app on your Ledger device to manage GO with MyCrypto or MyEtherWallet. The GoChain app is developed and supported by the GoChain community.

 GoChain accounts can not be added to Ledger Live.

Before you start

  • Set up Ledger Live with your Ledger device.
  • Update the firmware of your Ledger Nano S / Nano S Plus / Nano X.
  • Google Chrome or Firefox is installed.
  • The Ethereum app is installed since GoChain requires it.

Install the GoChain app

  1. Open My Ledger in Ledger Live.
  2. Connect and unlock your Ledger device.
  3. If asked, allow My Ledger to access your device.
  4. Find GoChain in the app catalog.
  5. Click the Install button of the app.
    • An installation window appears.
    • Your device will display Processing... 
    • The app installation is confirmed.

Next steps

  • Check the MyCrypto or MyEtherWallet user guides for setup instructions.
  • Reach out to the GoChain community on Reddit or Telegram if you need assistance.
