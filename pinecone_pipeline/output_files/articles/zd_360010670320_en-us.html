Woleet

Woleet is an app enabling Woleet ProofDesk users (paid service) to create electronic proofs of existence and timestamp documents on the Bitcoin blockchain. The Woleet app is developed and supported by Woleet. 

Before you start

  • Set up Ledger Live with your Ledger Nano S.
  • Update the firmware on your Ledger hardware wallet.

Install the Woleet app

  1. Open My Ledger in Ledger Live.
  2. Connect and unlock your Ledger device.
  3. If asked, allow My Ledger to access your device.
  4. Find Woleet in the app catalog.
  5. Click the Install button of the app.
    • An installation window appears.
    • Your device will display Processing...
    • The app installation is confirmed.
