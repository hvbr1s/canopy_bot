MIX Blockchain (MIX)

Install the MIX Blockchain app on your Ledger device to manage MIX with MyCrypto or MyEtherWallet. The MIX app is developed and supported by the Mix community.

 MIX accounts cannot be added to Ledger Live. Learn more about how to manage crypto assets with your Ledger device here.

Before you start

  • Set up Ledger Live with your Ledger device.
  • Update the firmware of your Ledger Nano S / Nano S Plus / Nano X.
  • Install the Ethereum app, as MIX requires it.

Install the MIX app

  1. Open My Ledger in Ledger Live.
  2. Connect and unlock your Ledger device.
  3. If asked, allow My Ledger to access your device.
  4. Find MIX in the app catalog.
  5. Click the Install button of the app.
    • An installation window appears.
    • Your device will display Processing... 
    • The app installation is confirmed.

Next steps

  • Check the MyCrypto or MyEtherWallet user guides for setup instructions.
  • Reach out to the MIX community on their Reddit or by email for support.
