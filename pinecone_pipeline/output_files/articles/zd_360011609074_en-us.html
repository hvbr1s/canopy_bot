GameCredits (GAME)

  GameCredits blockchain no longer active

GameCredits no longer supports the blockchain. You can still claim your ERC-20 tokens until the end of 2020. You can use the GameCredits app to claim your ERC-20 tokens. 

GameCredits is now an ERC20 token. You can manage ERC20 tokens in Ledger Live using the Ethereum app on your Ledger device. Up to 1,250 ERC-20 tokens are supported. Please refer to our list of supported crypto assets to see which ones.

For more information on how to manage GameCredits (GAME), refer to the following article: Manage ERC20 tokens. 

If you have any questions or requests about GameCredits (GAME), you can check the GameCredits contract page and reach out to info@gamecredits.org for technical support.
