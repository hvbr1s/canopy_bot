Validator (DASC)

  DasCoin Termination

Please note the following regarding DasCoin support:

  • DasWallet is not available anymore.
  • The DasCoin application is not available anymore from Ledger Nano S firmware version 1.6.0.

Install The Validator app on your Ledger hardware wallet to manage DASC tokens with DasWallet. The Validator app is developed and supported by the DasCoin community.

 DASC accounts can not be added to Ledger Live.

Before you start

  • Set up Ledger Live with your Ledger device.
  • Update the firmware on your Ledger hardware wallet.

Install The Validator app

  1. Open My Ledger in Ledger Live.
  2. Connect and unlock your Ledger device.
  3. If asked, allow My Ledger to access your device by pressing both buttons simultaneously.
  4. Find The Validator in the app catalog.
  5. Click the Install button of the app.
    • An installation window appears.
    • Your device will display Processing... 
    • The app installation is confirmed.

Next steps

  • Check the user guide for step-by-step instructions.
  • Reach out to DasCoin support by email or live chat if you require assistance.
