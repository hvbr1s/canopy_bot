Aeternity (AE)

Install the Aeternity app on your Ledger device to manage AE tokens with the Aeternity Base web app. The Aeternity app is developed and supported by the Aeternity community.

  Aeternity accounts cannot be added to Ledger Live.

Before you start

  • Set up Ledger Live with your device.
  • Update the firmware of your Ledger Nano S / Nano S Plus / Nano X.

Instructions

  1. Open My Ledger in Ledger Live.
  2. Connect and unlock your device.
  3. If asked, allow My Ledger to access your device.
  4. Find Aeternity in the app catalog.
  5. Click the Install button of the app.
    • An installation window appears.
    • Your device will display Processing... 
    • The app installation is confirmed.

Next steps

  • Check the Aeternity user guide for step-by-step instructions.
  • Reach out to the Aeternity community on their forum for support.
