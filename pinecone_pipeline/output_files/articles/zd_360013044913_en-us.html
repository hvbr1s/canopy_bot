ZClassic (ZCL)

Install the ZClassic app on your Ledger device to manage ZCL with Electrum-ZCL. The ZClassic app is developed and supported by the ZClassic community. Check the ZClassic wallet page to learn more.

  ZClassic accounts cannot be added to Ledger Live.

Before you start

  • Set up Ledger Live with your device.
  • Update the firmware of your Ledger Nano S / Nano S Plus / Nano X.

Instructions

  1. Open My Ledger in Ledger Live.
  2. Connect and unlock your device.
  3. If asked, allow My Ledger to access your device.
  4. Find ZClassic in the app catalog.
  5. Click the Install button of the app.
    • An installation window appears.
    • Your device will display Processing... 
    • The app installation is confirmed.

Next steps

  • Check the ZClassic user guide for step-by-step instructions.
  • Reach out to the ZClassic community on Reddit or GitHub for support.
