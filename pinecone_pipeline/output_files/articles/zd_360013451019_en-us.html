Liquid (L-BTC)

Install the Liquid app on your Ledger Nano S to manage L-BTC and other assets on the Liquid sidechain with Blockstream Green. 

 Liquid asset accounts cannot be added to Ledger Live.

 Liquid app only available on Ledger Nano S

Please note that the Liquid app is only available on the Ledger Nano S. Ledger Nano X and Ledger Blue don't support the Liquid app.

Before you start

  • Set up Ledger Live with your Ledger device.
  • Update the firmware on your Ledger Nano S.
  • Activate Developer mode in Ledger Live in Settings > Experimental features.

Install the Liquid app

  1. Open My Ledger in Ledger Live.
  2. Connect and unlock your Ledger device.
  3. If asked, allow My Ledger to access your device.
  4. Find Liquid in the app catalog.
  5. Click the Install button of the app.
