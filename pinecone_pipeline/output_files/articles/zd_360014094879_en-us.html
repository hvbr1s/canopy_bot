Export operation history as CSV

You can export your operation history in the Ledger Live desktop application as a CSV file.

  1. Click on Accounts on the left panel.
  2. Click on the ⋯ Options button in the upper right-hand corner.
  3. Choose the Export operation history option.

  4. Select the accounts to include in the window that appears.
  5. Click on Save.
  6. Save the file in a folder of your choice.
