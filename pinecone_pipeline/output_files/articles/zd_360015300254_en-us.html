REOSC (REOSC)

Install the REOSC app on your Ledger device to manage REOSC with MyCrypto or MyEtherWallet.

 REOSC accounts cannot be added to Ledger Live. Learn more about how to manage your crypto assets with your Ledger device here.

Before you start

  • Set up Ledger Live with your device.
  • Update the firmware of your Ledger Nano S / Nano S Plus / Nano X.
  • Install the Ethereum app, as REOSC requires it.
  • Get Google Chrome or Firefox.
  • Activate Developer mode in Ledger Live in Settings > Experimental features.

Install the REOSC app

  1. Open My Ledger in Ledger Live.
  2. Connect and unlock your Ledger device.
  3. If asked, allow My Ledger to access your device.
  4. Find REOSC in the app catalog.
  5. Click the Install button of the app.



    The REOSC app is developed and supported by the REOSC community.

Next steps

  • Check the MyCrypto or MyEtherWallet user guides for setup instructions.
  • Reach out to the REOSC community on Telegram if you need assistance.
