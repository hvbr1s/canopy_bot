Staking Algorand (ALGO) through Ledger Live

Important note regarding Algorand staking rewards

  • The Algorand Foundation has phased out participation (staking) rewards.
  • Starting from April 2022, staking rewards dropped to 0 and are replaced by governance rewards.
  • As a result, starting April 2022, you will stop receiving staking rewards in your Algorand account in Ledger Live.
  • To continue receiving rewards, you will need to sign up for the new Governance program (Become a Governor) via the Algorand Foundation website.
     Please note that Ledger Live does not support Governance Rewards. You will need to use a third party app like MyAlgo. 
  • You can learn how to sign up for the Governance program here.
