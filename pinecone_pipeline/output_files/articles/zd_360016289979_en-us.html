Kusama (KSM)

Install the Kusama app on your Ledger device to manage KSM with the Polkadot.js web wallet.

 Currently, it is not possible to create a Kusama account within Ledger Live. This action requires connecting your Ledger to a third-party application (Polkadot.js ).

'Txn version not supported' error

This error signifies that your Kusama (KSM) app is currently out of date. To continue using your account as usual, please connect your Ledger device to MyLedger via Ledger Live, and proceed to update the Kusama (KSM) app to version 23.9430.0 or above.

Before you start

  • Download and install Ledger Live on your computer. You can learn more about how to get started with Ledger Live here.
  • Open Ledger Live, connect your Ledger device to My Ledger, and make sure your Ledger device is running the latest firmware version.
  • Update the firmware of your Ledger Nano S / Nano S Plus / Nano X.

Install the Kusama (KSM) app on your Ledger device

I am a Ledger Nano X user
  1. Open Ledger Live and navigate to the My Ledger tab.
  2. Connect and unlock your Ledger device.
  3. If asked, allow My Ledger to access your device.
  4. Search for the Kusama app in the app catalog.
  5. Click the Install button of the app.
    • An installation window appears.
    • Your device will display "Loading, please wait..." 
    • The app installation is confirmed.
I am a Ledger Nano S user

For the Ledger Nano S, two versions of the Kusama app are available:

  • Kusama (KSM): the Kusama app allows you to send and receive KSM, bond and unbond KSM, and nominate validators. This app supports the most common features in Polkadot.js such as claiming rewards, tracking pending rewards, changing the rewards destination, etc. As of April 29th, 2022, the Kusama app size is 46 Kb but is subject to modification.
  • Kusama XL (KSM): the Kusama XL app is recommended for advanced users. Besides the main features in Polkadot.js, this app also supports advanced features in Polkadot.js such as voting, bounty program, validator settings, etc. As of April 29th, 2022, the Kusama XL size app size is 102 Kb but is subject to modification.

Depending on your needs, follow the steps below to install the Kusama app of your choice.

 The Kusama app is developed and supported by the Kusama community. Check the Kusama wallet page to learn more.
