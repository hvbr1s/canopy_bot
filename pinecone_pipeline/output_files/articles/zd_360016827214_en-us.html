Groestlcoin (GRS)

Install the Groestlcoin app on your Ledger device to manage GRS with Electrum-GRS. The Groestlcoin app is developed and supported by the Groestlcoin community.

GRS accounts cannot be added to Ledger Live. Learn more about how to manage crypto assets with your Ledger device here.

Before you start

  • Set up Ledger Live with your Ledger device.
  • Update the firmware of your Ledger Nano S / Nano S Plus / Nano X.

Install the Groestlcoin app

  1. Open My Ledger in Ledger Live.
  2. Connect and unlock your Ledger device.
  3. If asked, allow My Ledger to access your device.
  4. Find Groestlcoin in the app catalog.
  5. Click the Install button of the app.
    • An installation window appears.
    • Your device will display Processing...
    • The app installation is confirmed.
