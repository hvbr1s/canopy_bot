NIX Platform (NIX)

  NIX became MUTE token (ERC20) 

NIX moved away from its native blockchain and became an ERC-20 token in tandem with an accounted rebrand to Mute.io, with a dual token MUTE and VOICE ecosystem. The contract address of the MUTE token (ERC20) is 0xa49d7499271ae71cd8ab9ac515e6694c755d400c

The window for swapping NIX -> MUTE finished on July 5th, 2021.

As a result of this rebranding and migration to ERC20, the NIX app will be removed from My Ledger in Ledger Live.
You can manage the MUTE token with your Ethereum account. Learn more.

Install the NIX Platform app on your Ledger device to manage NIX with Electrum-NIX. The NIX app is developed and supported by the NIX community.

NIX accounts cannot be added to Ledger Live.

Before you start

  • Set up Ledger Live with your Ledger device.
  • Update the firmware on your Ledger hardware wallet.
  • The latest Bitcoin app is installed, as NIX requires it.

Install the NIX app

  1. Open My Ledger in Ledger Live.
  2. Connect and unlock your Ledger device.
  3. If asked, allow My Ledger to access your device.
  4. Find NIX in the app catalog.
  5. Click the Install button of the app.
    • An installation window appears.
    • Your device will display Processing...
    • The app installation is confirmed.
