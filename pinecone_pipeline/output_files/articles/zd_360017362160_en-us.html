Flow (FLOW)

Install the Flow app on your Ledger device to manage FLOW. The Flow app is developed and supported by the Flow community. Check the Flow wallet page to learn more.

 Flow accounts cannot be added to Ledger Live.

Before you start

  • Set up Ledger Live with your device.
  • Update the firmware of your Ledger Nano S / Nano S Plus / Nano X.

Install the Flow app

  1. Open My Ledger in Ledger Live.
  2. Connect and unlock your Ledger device.
  3. If asked, allow My Ledger to access your device.
  4. Find Flow in the app catalog.
  5. Click the Install button of the app.
    • An installation window appears.
    • Your device will display Processing... 
    • The app installation is confirmed.

How to connect

Flow users can install the Flow Nano app and connect directly to Flow Port by:

  1. Close Ledger Live.

  2. Connect your Nano device and unlock with your PIN code,

  3.  Open the Flow app on your Nano device.

  4. Go to Flow Port and click “Sign In”.

  5. Choose Ledger, and click Connect.

  6. Select your Nano from the pop-up box and click Connect.

  7. Select the account you wish to interact with.

    Thats it!
