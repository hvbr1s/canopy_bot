Terra 2.0 (LUNA)

This article will help you set up your first Ledger Terra 2.0 ($LUNA) account.

 The Terra app is developed and supported by the Terra community. Check the Terra wallet page to learn more. Create your first Ledger Terra account via the Terra Station web app.

  Terra Classic, Terra 2.0

  • On May 28, 2022, the Terra 2.0 chain went live and adopted the ticker $LUNA for its native coin.
  • The legacy Terra chain rebranded to Terra Classic and adopted the ticker $LUNC for its native coin.
  • To learn more about how to access your Terra Classic account and $LUNC balance, please click here.

Before you start

  • Download and install Ledger Live on your computer. You can learn more about how to get started with Ledger Live here.
  • Open Ledger Live, connect your Ledger device to My Ledger, and make sure your Ledger device is running the latest firmware version.
  • Update the firmware of your Ledger Nano S / Nano S Plus / Nano X.

Video instructions

Install the Terra app on your Ledger device

  1. Open Ledger Live and navigate to the My Ledger tab.
  2. Connect and unlock your Ledger device.
  3. If prompted, allow My Ledger to access your device.
  4. Search for the Terra app in the app catalog.
  5. Click the Install button to install the app on your Ledger device.
    • Your Ledger device displays Processing.
    • Ledger Live displays Installed. You're done.
