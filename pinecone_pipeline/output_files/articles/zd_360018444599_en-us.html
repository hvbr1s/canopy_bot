Connecting Ledger Live to DApps with WalletConnect

WalletConnect is an open-source protocol that allows you to securely connect mobile wallets to decentralized applications (DApps). You can use Wallet Connect to link Ledger Live to a wide range of DApps from desktop and mobile devices. You can consult the full list of decentralized applications supported by WalletConnect on this page. For full details on the integration of Wallet Connect into Ledger Live, please refer to our blog post.

In addition to WalletConnect, Ledger Live also offers direct access to a growing list of DApps in the Discover section. Check Discover in Ledger Live regularly as we will continue to add more dApps!

Wallet Connect Updates - March 5, 2023 - v2 just released! 

With the latest version (v2), you can now connect multiple accounts simultaneously. This means you no longer have to switch between accounts, saving you time and streamlining your experience.

Secondly, v2 also enables you to connect to multiple decentralized applications (dapps) at the same time. This new feature offers more flexibility and convenience, allowing you to access different dapps without disconnecting from others.

Lastly, we're happy to report that connection issues experienced in v1 have been resolved. Our team has worked hard to ensure that customers encounter fewer problems when using WalletConnect. As a result, you can enjoy a smoother and more seamless experience connecting to your accounts and dapps.

 However, please be aware that for a WalletConnect connection to function, the service or third-party app you wish to connect your Ledger to must support WalletConnect v2. If it doesn't, you won't be able to use WalletConnect and will need to explore alternative connection methods, such as MetaMask connected to your Ledger device.

Before you begin

  • Update Ledger Live through the notification banner or download the latest version.
  • Make sure your Ledger device has the latest firmware version: Ledger Nano S / Nano S Plus / Nano X.
  • Make sure your Ethereum/Polygon or Binance Smart Chain account holds some funds. 

 Supported Networks

  • Wallet Connect feature in Ledger Live is currently available for Ethereum, Polygon, and Binance Smart Chain accounts.

Connecting Ledger Live to DApps

 Important note

Please be aware that for a WalletConnect connection to function, the service or third-party dapp you wish to connect your Ledger to must support WalletConnect v2. If it doesn't, you won't be able to use WalletConnect and will need to explore alternative connection methods, such as MetaMask connected to your Ledger device.

Connecting to a DApp on desktop

  1. Go to the DApp of your choice.
  2. Connect to WalletConnect.
    A QR code appears.
  3. Copy the QR code to your clipboard.
  4. Open Ledger Live.
  5. Navigate to your Ethereum/Polygon or Binance Smart Chain account page.
  6. Click on the Manage button, then click on Wallet Connect.
    A Wallet Connect window appears.
  7. Paste the QR code you have copied to your clipboard. Then click Continue.
  8. Review the connection details, then click Continue.
    A confirmation window appears.

You have successfully connected Ledger Live Desktop to the dApp. You can now access the dApp on your desktop in a web browser.

Connecting to a DApp on mobile

  1. Go to the DApp of your choice on desktop
  2. Connect to WalletConnect. 
     A QR code is displayed on your screen. 
  3. Open Ledger Live Mobile. 
  4. Navigate to your Ethereum/Polygon or Binance Smart Chain account page.
  5. Tap on WalletConnect. 

  6. Scan the QR code displayed on your screen. 
  7. Review the connection details, then tap Connect.
    A confirmation window appears. 

You have successfully connected Ledger Live Mobile to a desktop DApp. You can now access the dApp on your desktop in a web browser.

Connecting to mobile DApp

  1. Go to the DApp of your choice on mobile. 
  2. Connect to WalletConnect.  
  3. Select the Ledger wallet.
    You are redirected to Ledger Live mobile. 

    The Select an Ethereum account page appears.
  4. Select the Ethereum account you want to connect to the DApp. 
  5. Verify the connection details then tap Connect. 


    A confirmation window appears. 

You have successfully connected Ledger Live Mobile to a mobile DApp. You can now access the dApp on your mobile in a web browser.
