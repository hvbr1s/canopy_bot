NEM (XEM)

Install the NEM app on your Ledger hardware wallet to manage XEM with NEM wallet. The NEM app is developed and supported by the NEM community.

  NEM accounts cannot be added to Ledger Live.

Before you start

  • Ledger Live is ready to use.
  • Update the firmware of your Ledger Nano S / Nano S Plus / Nano X.

Install the NEM app

  1. Open My Ledger in Ledger Live.
  2. Connect and unlock your Ledger device.
  3. If asked, allow My Ledger to access your device.
  4. Find NEM in the app catalog.
  5. Click the Install button of the app.
    • Your device will display Processing... 
    • The app will be installed.

Next steps

  • Check the NEM documentation for instructions.
  • Reach out to the NEM community on Telegram or by email: support@nem.group if you require assistance.
