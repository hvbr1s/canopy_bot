Symbol (XYM)

Install the Symbol app on your Ledger hardware wallet to manage XYM with Symbol Wallet. The Symbol app is developed and supported by the Nem community.

   Symbol accounts cannot be added to Ledger Live. Learn more about how to manage your crypto assets with your Ledger device here.

Before you start

  • Ledger Live is ready to use.
  • Update the firmware of your Ledger Nano S / Nano S Plus / Nano X.

Install the Symbol app

  1. Open My Ledger in Ledger Live.
  2. Connect and unlock your Ledger device.
  3. If asked, allow My Ledger to access your device.
  4. Find Symbol in the app catalog.
  5. Click the Install button of the app.
    • Your device will display Processing... 
    • The app will be installed.

Next steps

  • Check the Symbol user guide for step-by-step instructions.
  • Reach out to the NEM community on Telegram or by email: support@nem.group if you require assistance.
