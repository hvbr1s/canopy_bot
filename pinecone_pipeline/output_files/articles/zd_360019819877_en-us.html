IoTeX (IOTX)

Install the IoTeX app on your Ledger device to manage IOTX with ioPay Wallet App. The IoTeX app is developed and supported by the IoTeX community. Check the IoTeX wallet page to learn more.

 IoTeX accounts cannot be added to Ledger Live.

Before you start

  • Set up Ledger Live with your device.
  • Update the firmware of your Ledger Nano S / Nano S Plus / Nano X.

Install the IoTeX app

  1. In Ledger Live, go to Settings > Experimental features.
  2. Enable Developer mode.
  3. Go to My Ledger in Ledger Live.
  4. Connect and unlock your Ledger device.
  5. If asked, allow My Ledger to access your device.
  6. Find IoTeX in the app catalog.
  7. Click the Install button of the app.
    • Your device will display Processing... 
    • The app will be installed.

Next steps
