Celo (CELO)

Install the Celo app on your Ledger hardware wallet to manage CELO in Ledger Live. The Celo app is developed and supported by the Celo community.

Before you start

  • Make sure you have the latest version of Ledger Live installed. 
  • Update the firmware of your Ledger Nano S / Nano S Plus / Nano X.
  • Ensure you have the latest version of the Celo app on your Ledger device.
  • Enable Blind Signing in the Ethereum (ETH) app on your Ledger device.

Install the Celo app

  1. Open My Ledger in Ledger Live.
  2. Connect and unlock your Ledger device.
  3. If asked, allow My Ledger to access your device.
  4. Find Celo in the app catalog.
  5. Click the Install button of the app.
    • Your device will display Processing... 
    • The app will be installed.

Next steps

  • Add your first Ledger CELO (CELO) account in Ledger Live. Learn more here
  • Buy your Celo directly in Ledger Live with one of the dApps (MoonPay) in the Discover section.
  • Check the Celo user guide for step-by-step instructions on how to access the Celo app with the Ledger device.
  • Reach out to the Celo community on Reddit or on the Celo forum if you require assistance.
