Zilliqa (ZIL)

Install the Zilliqa app on your Ledger device to manage ZIL with the Zillet web wallet. The Zilliqa app is developed and supported by the Zilliqa community. Check the Zilliqa wallet page to learn more.

  Note that it is not recommended to use the Zilliqa app for token transfer unless you are an advanced user. 

 Zilliqa accounts cannot be added to Ledger Live.

Before you start

  • Set up Ledger Live with your device.
  • Update the firmware of your Ledger Nano S / Nano S Plus / Nano X.

Install the Zilliqa app

  1. Open My Ledger in Ledger Live.
  2. Connect and unlock your Ledger device.
  3. If asked, allow My Ledger to access your device.
  4. Find Zilliqa in the app catalog.
  5. Click the Install button of the app.
    • An installation window appears.
    • Your device will display Processing... 
    • The app installation is confirmed.

Next steps

  • Depending on your Ledger device, check one of the following Zilliqa user guides for setup instructions:
    • Ledger Nano S
    • Ledger Nano X
  • Reach out to the Zilliqa community on Discord, Telegram, Twitter, Reddit, Github, or Instagram.
