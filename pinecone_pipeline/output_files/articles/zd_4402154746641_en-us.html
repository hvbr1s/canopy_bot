Desmos (DSM)

Install the Desmos app on your Ledger device to manage DSM with Big Dipper. The Desmos app is developed and supported by the Desmos community. 

 Desmos accounts cannot be added to Ledger Live.

Before you start

  • Set up Ledger Live with your device.
  • Update the firmware of your Ledger Nano S / Nano S Plus / Nano X.

Install the Desmos app

  1. Go to the My Ledger in Ledger Live.
  2. Connect and unlock your Ledger device.
  3. If asked, allow the My Ledger on your device.
  4. Find Desmos in the app catalog.
  5. Click the Install button of the app.
    • Your device will display Processing... 
    • The app will be installed.

Next steps

  • Check the Desmos user guide for setup instructions.
  • Reach out to the Desmos community on Discord or Github for technical support.
