My parcel has been delivered to the wrong address, what should I do?

  • Please reach out to Ledger support.
  • In the email, make sure to include your order/tracking number and:

    • For DHL: please provide us with a scan or readable photo of your handwritten, signed statement indicating that you haven't received the parcel.

    • For Colissimo: please provide us with a scan or readable photo of your handwritten, signed statement indicating that you haven't received the parcel and a copy of your ID.

  • The carrier needs an identity document to compare your signature to the one on the delivery receipt.
  • Once we get this information we will submit a request to the carrier on your behalf.

Important information regarding claims

  • We won't be able to submit a claim with the carrier if you cancelled a signature requested or requested a delivery without signature .
  • Colissimo's average response time is 5 to 7 working days.
  • DHL's average response time is 1 to 3 working days.
  • All claims to DHL must be submitted within thirty (30) days of the date that DHL accepted the shipment.
