My parcel has arrived at an unknown pick-up point, what should I do?

  • First, go to the carrier's website and use your tracking number to locate your parcel.
  • If the address of the pick-up point is not available on the website, please reach out to Ledger support.
  • In the email, make sure to include your order tracking number.
  • Once we get this information we will ask the carrier to provide the address and we will forward it to you as soon as possible.

Important information regarding claims

  • Colissimo's average response time is 5 to 7 working days.
  • DHL's average response time is 1 to 3 working days.
  • All claims to DHL must be submitted within thirty (30) days of the date that DHL accepted the shipment.
