How can I update my delivery address?

If you selected DHL as the carrier, you can update the delivery address directly through the tracking link which was sent to your email address.

If you've selected a different carrier, navigate to the My Order page where you can modify your order, cancel it, download your invoice, and more.

Important information regarding claims

  • Collissimo's average response time is 5 to 7 working days.
  • DHL's average response time is 1 to 3 working days.
  • All claims to DHL must be submitted within thirty (30) days of the date that DHL accepted the shipment.

  • If you've selected Colissimo (standard delivery), you can't change your address once the order has been shipped. In this case, you can ask your local postal service to change your address or schedule a delivery to the nearest post office once your parcel has arrived in your country.
