My parcel has been returned to the sender, what should I do?

If your parcel has been returned to the sender, please contact Ledger customer support. 

In the email, make sure to include your order and/or tracking number.

Your order will be automatically refunded once the parcel arrives back at Ledger's warehouse in Vierzon. 

Tracking your parcel

  • Colissimo: No need to worry if you don't see any changes in the shipping status—when the parcel is in transit between France and your recipient country, the tracking status won't be updated until it reaches its destination.
  • DHL: If the parcel appears to be stalled or the delivery status is unclear, contact your local DHL office. If they are unable to solve the issue, contact Ledger support. 
  • You can find more information about delivery times by country and carrier here.
  • If your parcel gets lost, please contact Ledger support, and we will open a claim with the courier. Colissimo’s average response time to claims is 5 to 7 business days. DHL’s average response time to claims is 1 to 3 business days. DHL claims must be filed within 30 (thirty) days of the date the shipment was accepted.
