Connect your Ledger to Tron with TronScan

This tutorial describes how to connect your Ledger to Tron network with TronScan.

Before you start

  • Make sure the latest version of the Tron app is installed on your Ledger device.
  • Make sure to close Ledger Live as it can conflict with TronScan.

Connect your Ledger to Tron network with TronScan

  1. Connect your Ledger device and open the Tron app.
  2. Go to the TronScan website.
  3. Click Connect Wallet > Ledger > Connect.



    A list of accounts appears.
  4. Select an account and click Confirm.
  5. Verify and approve the address on your Ledger device.
    Your Ledger device connects to Tronscan.
  6. To access your Ledger account, click on your address in the top right-hand corner and select TRX Balances.



    Your Ledger Tron account appears.
  7. Click the copy icon to copy your Ledger Tron deposit address.
    Use this address to send TRX coins and TRC10/TRC20 tokens to your Ledger Tron account.
