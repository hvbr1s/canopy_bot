Clear cache in Ledger Live

Clear the cache in Ledger Live to fix an incorrect balance or operation history. This forces a full synchronization of your operation history with the blockchain network.

  Ledger Live Desktop   Ledger Live Mobile
  1. Go to the  Settings.
  2. Click on the Help tab.
  3. Find the Clear cache option.
  4. Click on Confirm after reading the instructions. The application will then reopen to your dashboard.
  1. Tap on the  Settings tab.
  2. Tap on the Help section.
  3. Choose Clear cache.
  4. Confirm by tapping Clear after reading the instructions. The application will then reopen to your dashboard.

Once Ledger Live has fully synchronized, your balance should be correct.
