Ledger Live interface overview

Ledger Live's interface makes it easy to find the features you need to manage your Ledger hardware wallet and the crypto assets it secures.

  Ledger Live Desktop   Ledger Live Mobile

Ledger Live's interface consists of four main elements:

  1. Application controls
  2. Menu
  3. Starred accounts
  4. Main viewer

1. Application controls 

Application control Icon Description
Network status The current status of synchronization with the blockchain networks of the crypto assets you've added. Click on it to force synchronization.
Information center

Keep track of the latest Ledger news and service status. 
Toggle discreet mode

Activate the discreet mode to hide your balances.
Help Get help from Ledger resources.
Lock Lock your session. Learn how to set up the password lock. 
Settings Access Ledger Live's settings.

2. Menu

Use the menu to go to different main sections:

  • Portfolio: Summary of your accounts.
  • Accounts: Manage your accounts.
  • Discover: Browse our web3 apps directly from Live's catalog.
  • Send: Send crypto assets at any time. Learn how to send crypto assets. 
  • Receive: Receive crypto assets at any time. Learn how to receive crypto assets. 
  • Buy / Sell: Buy and sell crypto assets. Learn more about buying and selling crypto assets. 
  • Swap: Swap crypto assets. Learn how to swap crypto assets. 
  • Lend: Lend assets on the Compound protocol and earn interest directly from your Ledger account. Learn more about lending crypto assets. 
  • My Ledger: Manage your Ledger device.

3. Starred accounts

The Starred accounts section features your favorite accounts for easy access. 

To add a starred account:

  1. In Ledger Live, go to Accounts. 
  2. Click on the star icon of the account you want to add as starred account.

4. Main viewer

The main viewer is the place where you interact with the main sections of Ledger Live. In Portfolio, the main viewer displays the following sections: 

  • Portfolio balance: a summary of your accounts, including the total balance, its countervalue, and the change over the selected period. 
  • Asset allocation: a summary of your asset allocation, including the percentage allocated to each asset, the amount, and its countervalue. 
  • Latest operations: a summary of your latest operations.

Tab menu 

At the bottom of the app, you'll find the Tab menu which provides access to different sections of the app.

  • Wallet: Summary of your accounts.
  • Market: Check the current situation on the crypto market directly from Ledger Live.
  • Transfer: Send, receive, swap, buy, sell crypto assets.
  • Discover: Safe and trusted place to explore and unlock the full potential of decentralized applications 
  • My Ledger: Manage your Ledger device.

Wallet

The Wallet tab shows a summary of your accounts, including the total balance, its countervalue, and the change over the selected period. On the top-right corner of the screen, you can access Settings and Notifications. At the bottom, in the middle, you can see the Transfer button.

Market

The Market tab gives you an overview of the situation on the cryptomarket. You can sort by market cap, and choose a different timeframe (24H / 7D / 30D / 1Y). By clicking on the asset, you will have detailed statistics of the asset's current situation like all-time high, trading volume, supply, and much more.

Discover

The Discover tab features the catalog of integrated applications and services in Ledger Live. It also features Learn and Earn sections that provide learning information on how to manage crypto, Ledger devices, NFTs, and DeFi.

My Ledger

The My Ledger tab features information about your Ledger device. In this section, you can manage the apps installed on your Ledger device, see the remaining storage and you can rename your Ledger device here.
