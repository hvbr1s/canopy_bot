Getting started with Ledger Live

The first time you use the Ledger Live application, it will help you set up your Ledger device and configure the app. Ledger Live stores your accounts and settings on your computer or phone. You will have to set up your accounts again on any additional computer or phone.

Before you begin

  • Get your Ledger Nano S or Ledger Nano X device.
  • Download and install Ledger Live.

Instructions

  Ledger Live Desktop   Ledger Live Mobile
Step 1. Get started
  1. Start the Ledger Live application.
  2. On the welcome screen, click on Get started.
  3. Choose how to set up your device:
    • Set up as new device to generate new private keys and to create new accounts. 
    • Restore device from recovery phrase to restore the private keys of your existing accounts using your (24-word) recovery phrase, also known as a mnemonic seed.
    • Connect your device: go to Step 3. Security checklist if your device is already set up.
  4. Choose your device, then click on Continue.
      The Ledger Nano X can only connect to the Ledger Live desktop app computer via USB.
Step 2. Set up your device

First, you'll choose a 4 to 8-digit PIN code that unlocks your device. Then you'll save or restore your 24-word recovery phrase. Your recovery phrase backs up the private keys that manage your crypto assets. It can be used to restore access to your crypto assets in case you lose access to your device.

  1. Set up your device by following the steps provided in Ledger Live:
    • To set up as a new device, check the dedicated article for Ledger Nano S or Ledger Nano X.
    • To restore your device from a recovery phrase, check the dedicated article.
  2. Once your Ledger hardware wallet is ready, click on Continue in Ledger Live.
Step 3. Security checklist

Complete the security checklist to verify that you benefit from the optimal level of security.

  1. Click on Check now, to verify that your device is a genuine Ledger device. Only a genuine Ledger device can provide the cryptographic proof required to connect with Ledger’s secure server.
       This process might take a few moments.
  2. Click on Continue once you see: Your device is genuine.
Step 4. Choose a password (optional)

Choose an optional password that unlocks the application to enhance your privacy. You should set a password if others have access to your computer.

  1. Click on Skip this step if you do not want to set a password. 
  2. Enter your password in the New password field.
  3. Enter your password again in the Confirm password field.
  4. Click on Continue.

   Make sure to remember your password. Losing it requires resetting Ledger Live and re-adding your accounts. This does not affect your crypto assets. 

Step 5. Bug reports & analytics

Besides mandatory technical data, you can choose which anonymous data to share with Ledger to help improve the quality of our products and services. By opting in, Ledger will automatically collect this data. You can decide to opt out at any time in the application settings.

  1. Select which optional data to share:
    • Analytics: Enable analytics of usage data to help Ledger improve the user experience.
    • Bug reports: Send automatic bug reports when the application crashes to help improve its stability.
  2. Click on Continue.
  3. Click on Open Ledger Live to open the application.

  To set up another device, restart the Device setup process from the Help tab in the settings.

Video instructions

Step 1. Get started

Ledger Nano X

  1. Open the Ledger Live app on your phone.
  2. Tap on Get started.
  3. Tap on Ledger Nano X.
  4. Choose your option:
    • Import desktop accounts: If your device is already set up and your accounts are in Ledger Live desktop. Proceed to the Import desktop accounts block below.
    • Initialize as new device: Go to Step 2. Set up your device.
    • Restore from recovery phrase: Continue to Step 2. Set up your device.
    • Connect your Nano if your device is already set up and ready to use.

Ledger Nano S & Ledger Blue

  1. Open the Ledger Live app on your phone.
  2. Tap on Get started.
  3. Choose your device.
  4. If your device is already set up and your accounts are in Ledger Live desktop: proceed to the Import desktop accounts block below. Otherwise, use Ledger Live desktop to set up your device.

  Import desktop accounts

  1. Tap Import desktop accounts.
  2. Start the Ledger Live desktop app, go to Settings > Export accounts and click Export.
  3. Tap Scan QR code on your phone and use it to scan the LiveQR code until the progress loader hits 100%.
  4. Select the accounts to import, choose to include general settings and tap Continue.

The accounts in Ledger Live mobile will now synchronize with the blockchain. Accounts do not synchronize between desktop and mobile.

Step 2. Set up your device
  1. Choose your option:
  • Set up as new device if this is your first time using a hardware wallet. Follow the instructions in Ledger Live mobile to choose your PIN code and write down your recovery phrase. Learn more
  • Restore from recovery phrase if you already have a recovery phrase to restore. Learn more
  • Once your device is ready, complete the security checklist in Ledger Live to verify that you benefit from the optimal level of security.
    Important: Contact us if you've answered No to any question, or when in doubt.
Step 3. Pair your Ledger Nano X
  1. Tap Pair new Ledger Nano X in Ledger Live. Bluetooth must be enabled on your phone and Ledger Nano X (enabled by default, turning Bluetooth on/off requires a reboot).

      On Android, Ledger Live requires location services to pair your device. Your location information is never stored by Ledger.
  2. Choose your Ledger Nano X once it's detected in Ledger Live. A Bluetooth pairing request with a pairing code is shown on both devices.
  3. Verify that both codes are the same, then confirm the pairing on your smartphone.
  4. Press both buttons on your Ledger Nano X to confirm the pairing.
  5. Press both buttons to allow My Ledger to access your Ledger Nano X. The pairing is complete after the genuine check with Ledger's secure server.
  6. Tap Rename to customize your Ledger Nano X device name.
Step 4. Set up password lock (optional)

Choose an optional password that unlocks the application to enhance your privacy. Depending on your phone's functionality, you can enable biometrics to unlock the app without entering your password.

  1. Tap Set password or Skip if you do not want to set a password. 
  2. Choose your password and tap Confirm.
  3. Enter your password once more.
  4. Tap the toggle to enable unlocking Ledger Live using your face or fingerprint.
  5. Tap Continue.

Make sure to remember your password. Losing it requires resetting Ledger Live and re-adding accounts. This does not affect your crypto assets.

Step 5. Bug reports & analytics

Besides mandatory technical data, you can share anonymous data with Ledger to help improve its products. Ledger will automatically collect this data unless you decide to opt out. This can be done at any time in Settings > General.

  1. Select which data to share:
    • Analytics: Enable analytics of usage data to help Ledger improve the user experience.
    • Bug reports: Send automatic bug reports when the application crashes to help improve its stability.
  2. Tap Continue to acknowledge having read and to accept Ledger's Privacy Policy and Terms of Use. 
  3. Tap Open Ledger Live to access Portfolio.
