Discover Live Apps

 Discover section no longer available on iOS mobile devices

Ledger Live version 2.37.2 removes the Ledger Live Discover section from iOS mobile to comply with Apple app store guidelines. The Discover section is still available on Android mobile devices and both desktop operating systems. These partners and features will be integrated into Ledger Live on iOS in the future in new and improved ways.

Discover is your safe and trusted place to explore and unlock the full potential of decentralized applications (dApps). Using dApps within Ledger Live ensures that you benefit from the highest level of security offered by your Ledger device.

  Note that Ledger doesn’t operate the dApps in the Live Apps Catalog, Ledger only provides an intuitive and secure interface to access them. Ledger is not responsible for any loss of funds or quality of service of such application. 

You can find below the list of the dApps currently available in Ledger Live. In case you require assistance using the dApps, you can also find the support channels for each dApp.
