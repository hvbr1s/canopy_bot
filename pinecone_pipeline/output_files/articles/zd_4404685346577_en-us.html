Toncoin (TON)

This article provides updated information about Telegram's Toncoin (TON) and the Ton network. You can learn more about the TON project here

What's Toncoin (TON)?

Toncoin is a cryptocurrency that is based on the Telegram Open Network (TON) blockchain which, according to the TON team, is able to to handle millions of transactions per second and support smart contracts.

Is Toncoin (TON) supported by Ledger?

Currently, there are two versions of Toncoin (TON) available:

  • As an ERC20 token on the Ethereum (ETH) network.
  • As the native coin of the TON network.

At present, Ledger devices do not support creating accounts on the TON network directly, nor does the Ledger Live app support the TON network. Consequently, we advise against sending TON to your Ledger account until the TON network is officially supported by Ledger.

Importantly, bridging the ERC20 version of Toncoin (TON) from Ethereum to the main TON network's native coin may result in TON funds being inaccessible until Ledger begins to support the TON network.

To store TON using your Ledger device, you can transfer Toncoin to your Ethereum (ETH) account as an ERC20 token from an exchange (ensure you select the Ethereum/ETH/ERC20 network during withdrawal). Once transferred, you can utilize the Zerion app within Ledger Live to view and manage your TON tokens.

If you wish to store TON tokens in your Ethereum account, please send a test amount first and verify that you're able to access your tokens before sending a larger balance.
