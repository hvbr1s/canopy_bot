Dock (DOCK)

Install the Dock app on your Ledger device to manage DOCK with Dock Node Frontend. The Dock app is developed and supported by the Dock community. 

 Dock accounts cannot be added to Ledger Live. Learn more about how to manage crypto assets with your Ledger device here.

Before you start

  • Set up Ledger Live with your device.
  • Update the firmware of your Ledger Nano S / Nano S Plus / Nano X.

Install the Dock app

Ledger Nano X Ledger Nano S
  1. Go to My Ledger in Ledger Live.
  2. Connect and unlock your Ledger device.
  3. If asked, allow My Ledger to access your device.
  4. Find Dock in the app catalog.
  5. Click the Install button of the app.
    • Your device will display Processing... 
    • The app will be installed.

For the Ledger Nano S, two versions of the Dock app are available:

  • Dock (DOCK): the Dock app allows you to send and receive DOCK, bond and unbond DOCK, and nominate validators. This app supports all the basic features on the Dock network. As of January 11th, 2022, the Dock app size is 42 Kb but is subject to modification.
  • Dock XL (DOCK): the Dock XL app is recommended for advanced users. Besides the basic functionalities, this app also supports advanced features such as governance (voting for proposals, becoming council member, etc) and identity features. As of January 11th, 2022, the Dock XL app size is 70 Kb but is subject to modification.

Depending on your needs, follow the steps below to install the Dock app of your choice.
