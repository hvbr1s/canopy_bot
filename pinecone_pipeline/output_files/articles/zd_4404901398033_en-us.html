Set up and use MyAlgo to access your Ledger Algorand (ALGO) account

This tutorial describes how to set up and use the MyAlgo web wallet with your Ledger device to access your Ledger Algorand (ALGO) account(s).

Before you start

  • Make sure the latest version of the Algorand (ALGO) app is installed on your Ledger device.
  • Make sure to close Ledger Live as it can conflict with MyAlgo.
  • Firefox users will face connectivity issues so we recommend using Chrome.

Accessing your Ledger Algorand account(s) via MyAlgo

  1. Go to https://wallet.myalgo.com/
  2. Select Access Now.



  3. Read the terms then accept and click Continue.



  4. On the website, accept the terms and click Continue.
  5. Choose and confirm a strong password and click Continue.
  6.  If you are prompted to change your password, simply click on Change password and follow the instructions.
    Please note that this password is only used to lock the MyAlgo interface for privacy reasons. Your ALGO remains securely protected by your Ledger device.



  7. Open the Algorand app on your Ledger device, then select Add Account > Ledger Nano.



  8. Select your device and click Connect.
    1. Select an account and click Access Account.



      Your Ledger Algorand account and ALGO balance appear.



      Use the Send and buttons to manage your account with MyAlgo and your Ledger device.
