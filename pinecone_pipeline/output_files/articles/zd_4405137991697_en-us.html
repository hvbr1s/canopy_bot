How safe are my assets if I’m using a third-party wallet such as Yoroi, Adalite, or Daedalus? 

In one sentence, they are just as safe as if you were managing your coins directly on Ledger Live. In both cases, your private keys never leave your Ledger device. Follow the steps outlined below to start managing ADA with a third-party wallet. 

Installing the Cardano app 

Before you can install the Cardano app, make sure you have set up Ledger Live with your device. 

  1. Open My Ledger in Ledger Live.
  2. Connect and unlock your Ledger device.
  3. If asked, allow My Ledger to access your device.
  4. Find Cardano in the app catalog.
  5. Click the Install button of the app.
    • An installation window appears.
    • Your device will display Processing...
    • The app installation is confirmed.

Managing your ADA with a third party wallet

Now that the Cardano application is installed on your device, make sure to close Ledger Live then head over to one of the following compatible third-party wallets

  • Adalite (Desktop only): check the Adalite user guide for step-by-step instructions.
  • Yoroi (Desktop and mobile): check the Yoroi user guide for step-by-step instructions.
  • Daedalus: note that this is a full node wallet and you will be required to download the entire Cardano blockchain history.

What's next?

Compatible third-party wallets are an essential part of the Ledger ecosystem and your assets are just as safe when you manage your Cardano through Adalite or Yoroi: your private keys remain on your Ledger device and they never leave it.

We do have plans to bring Cardano to Ledger Live though, we will communicate about them in due time.
