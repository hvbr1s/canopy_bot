Binance Smart Chain (BNB Chain)

This article will show you how to create your first Binance Smart Chain (BNB Chain) account and manage BNB and BEP-20 tokens with your Ledger device and Ledger Live. 

BEP-20 Tokens not displayed in Ledger Live due to visual bug

Currently, a visual bug may prevent BEP-20 tokens from displaying correctly within your Binance Smart Chain account in Ledger Live. Your funds are safe. We're actively working on a fix. In the interim, users affected by this issue can view and access their tokens through Zerion, available in the Discover section of Ledger Live.


Before you start

  • Download the latest version of Ledger Live via the Ledger website.
  • Update the firmware on your Ledger Nano S, Nano S Plus, or Nano X.

Install the Binance Smart Chain (BNB Chain) app on your Ledger device

  1. Open Ledger Live and navigate to the My Ledger tab.
  2. Connect and unlock your Ledger device.
  3. If asked, allow My Ledger to access your device.
  4. Search for the Binance Smart Chain (BNB) app in the app catalog.
  5. Click the Install button to install the app on your Ledger device.
    • Your Ledger device displays Processing.
    • Ledger Live displays Installed.



 The Binance Smart Chain (BNB Chain) app is developed and supported by Ledger.
