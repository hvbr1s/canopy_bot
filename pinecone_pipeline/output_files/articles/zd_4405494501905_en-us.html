Device stuck on Bootloader

If your device is stuck on Bootloader during the firmware update, follow these steps: 

Ledger Nano S and Nano S Plus Ledger Nano X
  1. Open Ledger Live.
    On Windows: Right-click Ledger Live and run as an Administrator.
  2. Connect your Ledger device.
  3. Go to the My Ledger tab of Ledger Live desktop and click on Continue.

    ​

  4. If the loader is at 100% and the device stays stuck on Bootloader: 
    Windows/Linux: Restart Ledger Live by pressing CTRL + R. Then start the repair tool once more. 
    MacOS: Restart Ledger Live by pressing ⌘ + R. Then start the repair tool once more.

If your device is stuck on Bootloader, try one of the following solutions:

  • Close and restart Ledger Live then go to My Ledger in Ledger Live.
  • Try on another computer.

Still not solved?

If your problem is still not solved, there is probably a connection issue:

  • Please check out this article to troubleshoot connection issues. 
  • If it still does not work, please reach out to Ledger Support and clearly describe all the steps you've tried.
