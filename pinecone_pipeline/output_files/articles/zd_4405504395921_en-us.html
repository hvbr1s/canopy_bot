Device displays: Update or Follow device update instructions

If your Ledger device displays "Update" or "Follow device update instructions" during the firmware update, it means that your device is ready for the last step of the update. 

  1. Disconnect the Ledger device.
  2. Go to My Ledger in Ledger Live.
  3. Press the button near the USB port of the Ledger device and keep it pressed while you reconnect the USB cable.
  4. Release the button when Bootloader is displayed on the device.
  5. The update process will continue automatically. Ledger Live will display multiple progress loaders, while your device displays Bootloader, Update, and Processing. Your device is successfully updated once Ledger Live displays Firmware updated.
