Firmware update not available in My Ledger

If the firmware update is not available in My Ledger, follow the steps below: 

  • You need Ledger Live on a computer to update your device. This firmware update is not available on the Ledger Live mobile app.
  • Make sure you are running the latest version of Ledger Live by going to Settings > About. You can download the latest version here.
  • If you still don't see the update in My Ledger, then it is not available to you yet. Please check again at a later moment, as the update is rolled out progressively.
