Edgeware (EDG)

Install the Edgeware app on your Ledger device to manage EDG with the Edgeware Wallet. The Edgeware app is developed and supported by the Edgeware community.

 Edgeware accounts cannot be added to Ledger Live. Learn more about how to manage crypto assets with your Ledger device here.

Before you start

  • Set up Ledger Live with your device.
  • Update the firmware of your Ledger Nano S / Nano S Plus / Nano X.

Install the Edgeware app

  1. Go to My Ledger in Ledger Live.
  2. Connect and unlock your Ledger device.
  3. If asked, allow My Ledger to access your device.
  4. Find Edgeware in the app catalog.
  5. Click the Install button of the app.
    • Your device will display Processing... 
    • The app will be installed.

Next steps

  • Check Edgeware user guide for setup instructions.
  • Reach out to the Edgeware community on Reddit or Telegram for technical support.
