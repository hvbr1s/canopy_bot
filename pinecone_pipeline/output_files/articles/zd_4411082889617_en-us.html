Moonriver (MOVR)

Install the Moonriver app on your Ledger device to manage MOVR with MetaMask. The Moonriver app is developed and supported by the Moonriver community. Check the Moonriver wallet page to learn more.

 Moonriver accounts cannot be added to Ledger Live.

Before you start

  • Set up Ledger Live with your device.
  • Update the firmware of your Ledger Nano S / Nano S Plus / Nano X.

Install the Moonriver app

  1. Go to My Ledger in Ledger Live.
  2. Connect and unlock your Ledger device.
  3. If asked, allow My Ledger to access your device.
  4. Find Moonriver in the app catalog.
  5. Click the Install button of the app.
    • Your device will display Processing... 
    • The app will be installed.

Next steps
