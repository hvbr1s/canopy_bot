Ledger Affiliate FAQs

This article compiles answers to the most common questions about Ledger Affiliate.

Becoming an affiliate

How do I become an affiliate?

Becoming a Ledger affiliate is easy:

  1. Sign up. Fill out the submission form and verify your identity.
  2. Share your affiliate link. Get your unique affiliate link and share it with your community.
  3. Get paid in BTC. Whenever a user makes a purchase using your affiliate link, you'll earn a commission in BTC. Once your account is validated, you can request payment. You will start with a 10% commission on every sale (net sale amount excluding VAT and shipping) and get more rewards as our partnership grows.
What’s the difference between Ledger’s affiliate and referral programs?

Refer a friend and Ledger Affiliate are two separate programs. Here are the main differences between them:

  Refer a friend Ledger Affiliate
Who is it for? It’s a friend invitation program, designed for Ledger users.  Ledger Affiliate is suitable for bloggers, YouTubers, influencers, and crypto companies that have a wide audience and want to educate about the importance of security and self-custody. 
Reward amount Refer a friend reward is $10 in Bitcoin to the referee. Ledger Affiliate reward starts with 10% affiliate commission for each sale (net sale amount, excluding VAT and shipping).
Reward limit Refer a friend bonus is limited to $1000 in Bitcoin per year.  Ledger Affiliate reward starts with a 10% affiliate commission on every sale, with more rewards available as our partnership grows. There is no limit to how many sales you can get paid commissions for.
Do I need to deposit funds to become an affiliate?

You don’t need to deposit funds to become an affiliate. However, you’ll need to earn at least 50 euros on your reward balance to withdraw your earnings.

How do I get a BTC address?

Affiliate earnings are paid out in BTC, so you will need an address to receive them. You can get a BTC address using the Ledger Live app or through a crypto exchange such as Coinbase or Kraken.

Who is not eligible to become a Ledger affiliate?

Ledger Affiliate guidelines don't allow:

  • Participating in both the reseller and affiliate program
  • Participating in both the referral and affiliate program
  •  Illicit traffic sources such as discount/coupon sites, cashback platforms, and sub-affiliate networks.

We encourage you to take a close look at our Terms and Conditions to learn more about Ledger Affiliate guidelines and restrictions.

Managing your affiliate account

How do I validate my account?

Log in to your account and scroll down to the Your Profile Information section. Enter and submit the required information, for example:

  1. Your main social media platform and primary source of traffic, where you intend to share your unique affiliate link.
  2. KYC information, such as a valid ID and proof of residence.

Our team will review your application and notify you once your account is validated.

When do I get my affiliate link?

You’ll get access to your dashboard and affiliate link as soon as you sign up.

How can I view my affiliate earnings?

You can view your earnings in your dedicated affiliate dashboard.

How can I get the affiliate link for the latest promotion?

 Here are the steps:

  1. Copy the link of the latest promotion. For example: https://shop.ledger.com/products/crypto-starter-pack
  2. Paste ‘?r=YOURReference’ at the end of the link. Just make sure to change it to your unique uuid.

This is how the final link should look like: https://shop.ledger.com/products/crypto-starter-pack?r=YOURREFERENCE

How do I get my Ledger Nano S Plus affiliate link?

You can get your Ledger Nano S Plus affiliate link by adding your custom REFERENCE to the end of the link. For example, if your UUID is: ’?r=1a2b3c’ 

Your Ledger Nano S Plus link would be: https://shop.ledger.com/products/ledger-nano-s?r=1a2b3c

Can I change the BTC address where my affiliate earnings are sent?

The steps are as follows:

  1. Log in to your affiliate dashboard.
  2. Scroll down until you reach the Your Account section.
  3. Click Edit Profile.
  4. Navigate to the BTC payout address field and enter your new address.
  5. Click Save.

Keep in mind that the status of your account will be changed to Pending validation. It will take around 2 weeks to have your account validated.

Getting your affiliate commissions

When will I receive my affiliate commission payment?

Payments are usually sent every Monday at noon (CET). To receive payment, you must submit a request and be eligible for it.

Can I receive payments in US dollars or other fiat currency?

Ledger Affiliate doesn't offer payments in fiat currencies. However, you can sign up for our Impact program and get paid in US dollars.

Can I withdraw my affiliate earnings even though my balance is lower than 50 euros?

If your balance is below the minimum threshold (50 euros), you won't be able to withdraw your earnings.

Why is my account balance negative?

Your commission was most likely earned on a canceled order. Commissions may be reverted within 30 days if the corresponding order is canceled by the user.

My affiliate account wasn’t credited after the sale. How should I proceed?

Using a browser that blocks cookies can sometimes result in commissions not being correctly assigned to affiliates. Recommend your community to use a browser with enabled cookies. Unfortunately, the commission can’t be added after the order is placed.

A Ledger product I recommended was purchased without using my affiliate link. Can I still get a commission?

Unfortunately, no. Affiliate commissions are only earned when users make a purchase through your unique affiliate link.
