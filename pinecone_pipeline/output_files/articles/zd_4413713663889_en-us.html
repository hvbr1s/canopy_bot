Activating the discreet mode in Ledger Live

Discreet mode allows you to manage your crypto assets in Ledger Live, without revealing any sensitive account data. Once the discreet mode is enabled, your balance totals and transaction amounts will be hidden from prying eyes.

  Ledger Live Desktop   Ledger Live Mobile
  1. Open Ledger Live.
  2. Click the eye icon   in the top right corner of the screen.

You have successfully activated the discreet mode. Your balance totals and transaction amounts are replaced by asterisks.

To return to the normal view, click the eye icon once again.
