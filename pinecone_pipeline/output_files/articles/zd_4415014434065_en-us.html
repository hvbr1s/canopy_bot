Shyft (SHFT)

Install the Shyft app on your Ledger device to manage SHFT with MetaMask. The Shyft app is developed and supported by the Shyft community.

 Shyft accounts cannot be added to Ledger Live.

Before you start

  • Set up Ledger Live with your device.
  • Update the firmware of your Ledger Nano S / Nano S Plus / Nano X.

Install the Shyft app

  1. Go to My Ledger in Ledger Live.
  2. Connect and unlock your Ledger device.
  3. If asked, allow My Ledger to access your device.
  4. Find Shyft in the app catalog.
  5. Click the Install button of the app.
    • Your device will display Processing... 
    • The app will be installed.

Next steps

  • Check the Shyft user guide for setup instructions.
  • Reach out to the Shyft community on Telegram for technical support.
