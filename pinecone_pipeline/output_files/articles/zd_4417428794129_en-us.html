How should I prepare a return package with a prepaid label?

Learn how to properly prepare and label your package so your parcel arrives in good condition and on time.

Before you start

  • Make sure you have received a prepaid label from Ledger support before you send back the defective device. 
  • Reset your device from its settings menu or by entering three incorrect PIN codes when unlocking it. For step-by-step instructions, refer to this article: Reset to factory settings.

  • Use boxes that are no larger than double the size of the Nano device package.

Preparing your return package

      Don’t include your 24-word recovery phrase in the return package.
  2. Print your prepaid label and stick it on your parcel.
  3. Schedule the pickup of your parcel or drop it at any DHL point.

  2. Print the PDF document sent by our Support team:
  • Page 1: Stick the prepaid shipping label on the package.
  • Page 2: Give this document to DHL during the pickup of your package or the drop of your package at the DHL point.
  • Page 3, 4, 5: Insert the three identical invoices inside an adhesive transparent envelope and attach the envelope to the parcel. This gives the customs authorities a clear overview of what you are shipping and helps prevent delays or even fines.
  • Schedule the pickup of your parcel or drop it at any DHL point.

    You have the option to either schedule a pickup for your parcel or drop it off at the nearest DHL point, which you can locate here based on your current location.

    To schedule a pickup with DHL, please follow these steps:

    1. Access to MyDHL+ service (Make sure to select your country in the top right corner by clicking on the flag).

    2. Click on the "Schedule a pickup" button.

    3. When asked "Do you need to create a shipping label?" Please answer "No"

    4. Copy and paste your waybill number: XXXXXXXXXX (This number can be found on the prepaid label generated that you printed (“returndocs.pdf”).

    5. Enter your phone number

    Depending on where you are located, it may not be possible to schedule a pickup for your package, and you may need to drop it off at a designated pickup point.
