Connect your Ledger to Binance Smart Chain (BNB) with Metamask

This tutorial describes how to access your Ledger Binance Smart Chain (BNB) account via MetaMask.

Before you start

  • Make sure to update Ledger Live to the latest version via this link.
  • Open Ledger Live and connect your Ledger device to My Ledger to ensure that your device is running the latest firmware version.
  • Install the latest version of the Ethereum (ETH) app on your Ledger device
  • Make sure to also install the latest version of the Binance Smart Chain (BNB) app on your Ledger device.


Accessing your Ledger Binance Smart Chain account(s) via MetaMask.

  1. Close Ledger Live.
  2. Go to https://metamask.io/ to download and install Metamask to your browser.
  3. Follow these steps to connect your Ledger to Metamask.
  4. Once connected, open the network selection menu and click Add network.



  5. Navigate to the BNB Smart Chain option and click Add.



  6. Click Approve.



  7. Click Switch to BNB Smart Chain to confirm the connection.



    Your Ledger Binance Smart Chain account and BNB balance appear in the app.
    You're all done!


 

 I can't see my BEP20 tokens!

  • Unlike Ledger Live, which automatically displays supported token balances in your account, most tokens need to be manually added to Metamask in order to be visible.
  • If a token balance has not been added to Metamask, it will remain hidden. To add a custom token, please follow these steps.
