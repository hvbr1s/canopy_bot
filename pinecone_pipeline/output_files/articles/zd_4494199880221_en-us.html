Technical questions

What are the requirements to use Ledger Nano S Plus?

Ledger Nano S Plus requires a USB port to connect the USB-C cable to. The device requires a desktop computer with at least Windows 10, macOS 10.15 (64-bit), or Linux Ubuntu 20.04 (64-bit) or a smartphone with at least Android 10.

How many apps can be installed on the device?

Ledger Nano S Plus supports up to 100 simultaneously-installed apps. It has five times more available storage space for apps compared to Ledger Nano S, though the number of apps that can be installed depends on the size of the apps.

Why can't I find an app for Ledger Nano S Plus in My Ledger?

Certain apps might not be available for downloading on Ledger Nano S Plus in My Ledger.

Below mentioned apps are not available in My Ledger. The deployment of these apps for Nano S Plus can be delayed.

  • InternetComputer
  • IOTA Legacy

These apps will be available for Ledger Nano S Plus within the shortest delay. While waiting, if you are the owner of Ledger Nano S or Nano X, you can install the mentioned apps on those devices.
