Managing your Solana NFT collection with Magic Eden

This tutorial describes how to connect your Ledger Solana (SOL) account to Magic Eden via the Phantom app.

Before you start

  • Make sure you're running the latest version of Ledger Live or update via this link.
  • Connect your Ledger device to My Ledger and ensure that your device runs the latest firmware version. You can learn more here (Nano S users), here (Nano X users), and here (Nano S Plus users).
  • Ensure the latest version of the Solana app is installed on your Ledger device.
  • Ensure blind signing is enabled in the Solana app on your Ledger device. You can learn how to do this here

Video instructions

Connecting your Ledger Solana (SOL) account to Magic Eden

  1. Open your browser and unlock the Phantom app.
  2. Connect your Ledger Solana account to the Phantom app using this tutorial.
  3. Navigate to the Magic Eden website.
  4. On the website, click Select Wallet > Phantom
    Your Ledger Solana account connects to Magic Eden.
     If you're not getting a prompt to connect your Ledger account via Phantom, please refresh the page and try again.
  5. Click the button located right to Sell button to access your dashboard.
  6. Select My items to access your Solana NFTs.

 Buying and Selling NFTs on Magic Eden

  • You can trade Solana NFTs on Magic Eden directly from your Ledger Solana account.
  • To learn more, please consult Magic Eden's documentation here.
