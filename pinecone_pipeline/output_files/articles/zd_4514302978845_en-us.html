Nodle (NODL)

This tutorial will show you how to create your first Ledger Nodle (NODL) account with your Ledger device and Nodle Cash.
 The Nodle app is developed and supported by the Nodle community.

Before you start

  • Set up Ledger Live with your Ledger device.
  • Update the firmware on your Ledger Nano S, Nano S Plus, or Nano X.

Install the Nodle (NODL) app on your Ledger device

  1. Connect and unlock your Ledger device.
  2. Open Ledger Live and navigate to My Ledger.
  3. If asked, allow access to My Ledger by pressing both buttons on your Ledger device simultaneously. 
  4. In My Ledger, select the App catalog, search for Nodle (NODL), and click Install.
    You're done.
