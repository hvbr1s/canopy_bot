How to submit a personal data request?

When you use our services, we may collect some of your personal information. 

If you want to make a request regarding your data (a deletion request or an access request for instance) or ask us any related question, you can fill in the form available here:

https://privacy-request.ledger.com/

Please note that inquiries not related to personal data won’t be answered and that you will be asked to contact our support team.
