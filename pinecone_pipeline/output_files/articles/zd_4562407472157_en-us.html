Claim your Ledger Nano S Plus Genesis NFT

Ledger Nano S Plus Genesis is a special edition of the Nano S Plus. This special edition comes with a custom engraved swivel and a POAP card that allows you to get an exclusive NFT.


POAP stands for Proof Of Attendance Protocol. Like a digital memento, POAPs are NFTs you can collect by attending important cultural events to commemorate the occasion. Learn more about POAP in this Ledger Academy article.

Before you start

  • Make sure you have your ETH address ready. To obtain your ETH address, refer to this article.

Claim your Ledger Nano S Plus Genesis NFT

The Ledger Nano S Plus Genesis includes a POAP card. On the back of this card, you will find a QR code.

(POAP card included in the Ledger Nano S Plus Genesis Edition box)

To claim this exclusive NFT, do the following:

  1. Scan the QR code on the back of this card with your phone camera.
     If you’re unable to scan the QR code, go to the link located on the bottom right corner of the back of the card.
    You are redirected to a POAP Claim page.
  2. Enter your Ethereum address.
    Note: By default, POAP NFTs are minted for free using the fast and environmentally-friendly xDAI blockchain. We recommend leaving the “Free minting in xDAI” checkbox checked. You can always migrate it to Ethereum mainnet later on.
  3. Click on the Mint POAP token button.
  4. Wait for a few seconds while your POAP is being added to your collection.

You have successfully added the Ledger Nano S Plus Genesis Edition POAP to your collection!

View your Ledger Nano S Plus Genesis NFT

You can view your Ledger Nano S Plus Genesis NFT in Ledger Live, on the POAP website, or the POAP mobile app available for iOS and Android. 

 POAP app no longer available for iOS users

Ledger Live version 2.37.2 removed the Discover section from iOS mobile to comply with Apple app store guidelines. Since the POAP app was part of the Discover section, iOS users can't view this NFT using the POAP app in Ledger Live mobile. The POAP app will be integrated into Ledger Live on iOS in the future in new and improved ways.

To view your Ledger Nano S Plus Genesis NFT in Ledger Live: 

  1. Open Ledger Live.
  2. Navigate to the Discover tab.
  3. Select the POAP app.
  4. At the top-left corner, select the Ethereum account you used to mint the POAP token.

Your token collection appears and you can view your brand new Ledger Nano S Plus Genesis NFT!

Can’t claim your Ledger Nano S Plus Genesis NFT?

If you are unable to claim your Ledger Nano S Plus Genesis NFT, please contact our support team by using the Help widget located at the bottom right of the page.
