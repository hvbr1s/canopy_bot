How to access your Ledger Celo account via Celo Wallet

This tutorial describes how to access your Ledger Celo account via the Celo wallet. You can learn more about Celo here.

Before you start

  • Make sure to update Ledger Live to the latest version via this link.
  • Open Ledger Live and connect your Ledger device to My Ledger to ensure that your device is running the latest firmware version.
  • Ensure that the latest version of the Celo (CELO) app is installed on your Ledger device.
  • Once you're all done, close Ledger Live as it can conflict with Celo wallet.

Access your Ledger Celo account via Celo wallet

  1. Go to the Celo wallet website.
  2. Choose Use existing account.
  3. Select Use Ledger.
  4. Connect your Ledger and open the Celo application.
  5. Select address index (0 by default).
  6. Proceed to Import account.
    A pop-up window will appear in your browser
  7. Select your Ledger device and click Connect.

    Celo wallet dashboard will open and then you should be able to see your wallet balance and perform transactions now.

Next steps

  • Check the Celo documentation for detailed instructions on how to use the Celo wallet with your Ledger device.
  • Reach out to the Celo community on Reddit or on the Celo forum if you require assistance with Celo wallet.
