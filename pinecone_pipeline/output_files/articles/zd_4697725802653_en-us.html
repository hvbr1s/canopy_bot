Hydra (HYDRA)

Install the Hydra app on your Ledger device to manage HYDRA with the Hydra web wallet. The Hydra app is developed and supported by the Hydra community. 

 Hydra accounts cannot be added to Ledger Live.

Before you start

  • Set up Ledger Live with your device.
  • Update the firmware of your Ledger Nano S / Nano S Plus / Nano X.

Install the Hydra app

  1. Open My Ledger in Ledger Live.
  2. Connect and unlock your Ledger device.
  3. If asked, allow My Ledger to access your device.
  4. Find Hydra in the app catalog.
  5. Click the Install button of the app.
    • An installation window appears.
    • Your device will display Processing... 
    • The app installation is confirmed.

Next steps

  • Check the Hydra user guide for setup instructions.
  • Reach out to the Hydra community on Telegram or with the contact form if you require assistance.
