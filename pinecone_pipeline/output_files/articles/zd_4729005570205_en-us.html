How to access your Terra 2.0 ($LUNA) account via Terra Station

This tutorial describes how to access your Terra 2.0 ($LUNA) account via Terra Station.

 You can learn more about the features supported by Terra Station here.

 Before you go further

  • This tutorial describes how to access your new Ledger Terra 2.0 account and $LUNA balance.
  • To learn how to access your Terra Classic account and $LUNC balance, please click here.

Before you start

  • Make sure to update Ledger Live to the latest version via this link.
  • Open Ledger Live and connect your Ledger device to My Ledger to ensure that your device is running the latest firmware version.
  • Ensure that the latest version of the Terra app is installed on your Ledger device.
  • Once you're all done, close Ledger Live as it can conflict with Terra Station.
  • We recommend using Chrome or Brave browser.

Video instructions

Accessing your Ledger Terra 2.0 account via Terra Station Wallet extension

  1. Connect your Ledger device to your desktop computer and open the Terra app on your device.
    Your device displays: Terra ready.
  2. Download the Terra Station Wallet browser extension here.
  3. Click on the extension icon to open it in your browser.
  4. Select Access with ledger then click Connect.
    Your Ledger Terra account address and $LUNA balance appears in the app.
  5. Click the copy icon next to your address to copy it to your clipboard.
    You can safely send $LUNA to this address.


Send $LUNA out of your Ledger Terra 2.0 account via Terra Station web app

  1. Go to the station.terra.money web app.
  2. In the web app, select Connect > Terra Station Wallet.
    The web app displays: Connect to application.
  3. Click Connect.
    Your Ledger Terra account connects to the web app.
  4. In the web app, select Wallet and navigate to your $LUNA balance.
  5. Click Send.
  6. Chose the recipient's address, the amount, and click Submit.
  7. Review the transaction and click Post.
    Your Ledger device displays: View transaction.
    Terra Station displays: Confirm in ledger.
  8. On your Ledger device, review the transaction, and if all looks good press both buttons to Sign Transaction.
    Terra Station displays: Broadcasting transaction then Success!
  9. Click Confirm.
    You're done.
