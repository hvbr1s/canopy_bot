Arweave (AR)

This article describes how to create your first Arweave (AR) account with your Ledger Nano S or Nano S Plus and Arweave Web Wallet.

Visit the Arweave website to learn more about the project. 

 Note to Nano X users

Currently, the Arweave feature is only available to Nano S and Nano S Plus users and not Nano X users. Our team is actively working on bringing this feature to Nano X users as quickly as possible.

Before you start

  • Download and install the latest version of Ledger Live via this link.
  • Make sure your Ledger device runs the latest firmware. Learn more.

Video instructions

Install the Arweave app on your Ledger device

  1. Open Ledger Live.
  2. Go to Settings > Experimental features and activate Developer mode.



  3. Go to My Ledger.
  4. Connect and unlock your Ledger device.
  5. If asked, allow My Ledger on your Ledger device.
  6. Find Arweave in the app catalog.
  7. Click the Install button of the app.



Connect your Ledger to the Arweave network via Arweave Web wallet

Creating an Arweave account directly in Ledger Live is currently not supported. To set up an account, you'll need to connect your Ledger Nano S or Nano S Plus device to a third-party application, the Arweave Web Wallet.

  1. Go to https://arweave.app/welcome
  2. Using the buttons, open the Arweave app on your Ledger device. Your device screen should display the words: Pending ledger review.
  3. Press both buttons simultaneously a second time to open the app. Your device screen should display the words Arweave Not Ready.



  4. Using the right button, scroll right until your device displays the words Click to initialize then press buttons simultaneously again to initialize your keys.



     The initialization process may take a few minutes. Your device will be fully initialized when the screen displays the words Arweave ready.

  5. Once your device is ready click the [+] button and select Connect with Ledger.
  6. Your Ledger account and deposit address appear in the app.
    Click Finish.



  7. Click the copy button to copy your Ledger Arweave account address. You can use this address to send AR coins to your Ledger wallet.
