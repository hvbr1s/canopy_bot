How to use the Cryptotag

Your 24-word Recovery phrase serves as a backup to your crypto assets. As long as you have your Recovery phrase, you will always be able to regain access to your crypto assets. 

You can use the Cryptotag to add an extra layer of security to your crypto assets. Cryptotag allows you to store your Recovery Phrase in 6mm-thick titanium storage that is designed to be waterproof, fireproof, corrosion-proof, hackerproof, and bulletproof. 

Secure your Recovery Phase with Cryptotag

  1. Write down your 24 recovery words on the included Conversion Sheet. 
  2. Find the correlating numbers in the BIP39 word list.
      You can find the BIP39 word list in the Cryptotag setup guide.
  3. Write down the numbers on the Conversion Sheet.
  4. Punch the numbers in the Cryptotag Plates.
      Make sure to always double-check the numbers on the Conversion Sheet. Once you push the numbers in the Cryptotag Plates, you won't be able to make any changes.
  5. Connect the plates and store your Cryptotag in a safe place.
  6. Burn the Conversion Sheet.

For step-by-step instructions, you can check the tutorial video created by Cryptotag.
