Wethio (ZYN)

Install the Wethio app on your Ledger device to manage Zynecoin with Wethio Web Wallet. The Wethio app is developed and supported by the Wethio community. 

 Wethio accounts cannot be added to Ledger Live.

Before you start

  • Set up Ledger Live with your device.
  • Update the firmware of your Ledger Nano S / Nano S Plus / Nano X.

Install the Wethio app

  1. Open My Ledger in Ledger Live.
  2. Connect and unlock your Ledger device.
  3. If asked, allow My Ledger to access your device.
  4. Find Wethio in the app catalog.
  5. Click the Install button of the app.
    • An installation window appears.
    • Your device will display Processing... 
    • The app installation is confirmed.

Next steps

  • Check the Wethio guide for setup instructions.
  • Reach out to the Wethio community on Telegram or by email: support@zynecoin.io if you require assistance.
