Solve error 0x6E01

This article explains how to solve the 0x6E01 error that occurs when you try to connect with third-party apps. The 0x6E01 error is caused by not launching the app on your device at the right moment. 

How to solve error 0x6E01?

To solve this error, make sure that you open the app before trying to connect with third-party services or platforms. 

Are you still getting errors?

If you continue to see the 0x6E01 error, please contact our Support team.
