CL Card FAQ

This article compiles answers to the most common questions about the CL Card Powered by Ledger.
CL Card is offered in partnership with Baanx.

Having CL Card issues? Please contact Baanx directly at: support@cl-cards.com.

General questions 

Do I need a Ledger wallet and/or Ledger Live to get or use the CL Card?
You don’t need a Ledger wallet and/or Ledger Live to get or use the CL Card. However, if you want to enhance the security of your CL Card user experience, we highly recommend using your CL Card with your Ledger wallet. By combining your Ledger wallet with the Ledger Live app, you can seamlessly and securely move your assets between your Ledger wallet and the CL Card, taking advantage of Ledger’s unique digital security ecosystem.
What does Powered by Ledger stand for?

CL Card is operated by Baanx.* Powered by Ledger stands next to its name because the card is fully integrated with Ledger’s ecosystem. You can create a CL account in Ledger Live and move assets seamlessly and securely between your Ledger wallet and the card.

Can I use my crypto as collateral?

Yes, you can use Cryptodraft or Stableloan and open a line of credit to spend on your card.**** 

Can I earn cashback with my CL Card?

Every time you spend with your CL Card, you get rewards.****
To enable rewards, you first need to select the crypto you want to receive cashback in—currently available in BTC, USDT, and BXX. The reward amount will be calculated based on all the transactions you've made with your card throughout the month. At the end of each month, you will receive a 1% reward for the total amount of card transactions (BTC, USDT) or a 2% reward if you choose BXX.

Is the crypto-to-fiat conversion carried out directly from my Ledger wallet?

No, you need to fund your CL Card before you start spending. You can fund the card with the crypto you have in your Ledger Live wallet. Crypto-to-fiat conversion will take place at the time of purchase.***

How do transfers of funds work from a self-custody perspective?

You have total ownership over your assets as long as their private keys are safely stored in your Ledger wallet. Once you transfer funds from your wallet to the card, they are no longer under self-custody.

Can I add my CL Card to Apple Pay?

Yes, adding your CL Card to Apple Pay is easy. Check out these FAQs to learn more about using Apple Pay with your CL Card.

Managing CL Card

What can I do with my CL Card?

The CL Card can be used for online purchases and payments.

How do I activate my card?

Once your card arrives, go to your CL account in the Ledger Live app and activate it by entering the 3-digit CVV code— it’s located at the back of the card.

My CL Card is activated, but I am not able to make payments with it. Why?

You need to use the PIN code when you make the first payment with your card. You can see your PIN in the Ledger Live app.

What types of payments can I make with my card?

The card supports recurring payment authorizations, however, it doesn't allow you to set up a standing order or check your balance at ATMs. Transactions requiring pre-authorization, such as those at gas stations, may also be unavailable.

My account balance shows 0 when I check it at an ATM even though I have funds. Why?

An ATM will display 0 for any balance inquiries. You can see your card's correct balance in your CL account.

What is the maximum number of CL wallets that I can link to my card?

CL platform allows you to have up to 5 wallets, one per crypto. This means that you can link a maximum of 5 wallets to your card.

How is the available balance for my card calculated?

The available balance displayed for your card is an estimate. We dynamically calculate the total fiat value of your linked crypto wallets + the sum of your assets against live market rates, and then we apply our liquidation fee percentage.

What are the prerequisites for ordering a CL Card?

Before ordering your CL Card, you need to pass a KYC and account verification process. You also need to ensure that you have enough funds in your account. In addition, you must reside in one of the supported countries.

What are the limitations of the CL Card?

Navigate to the Manage Card tab and check the User restrictions section for more information.

I haven't received my card yet. Why?

It may take up to two weeks to receive your card. If you haven't received it after this time, please contact support@cl-cards.com or raise a ticket via live chat.

My card is lost, stolen or damaged. Can I get a replacement?

If your card has been lost, damaged or stolen, please log in to your account and navigate to Settings. You'll see the option to freeze your card and report it as lost, stolen or damaged. After that, you can order a replacement card.

What should I do if my card is blocked?

Your card may be blocked if you reported it as lost, damaged or stolen. Please contact support@cl-cards.com if this is not the case or if you need further assistance.

Why is my card not working at merchant terminals?

If your card is not working at merchant terminals, it may have been blocked. Please go to an ATM and select PIN SERVICES → UNBLOCK PIN. You should wait at least 15 minutes before attempting another transaction.

I forgot my PIN code. Where can I view it?

If you forgot your PIN code, log in to your CL account and select Settings. Your PIN will be displayed there.

How can I deposit crypto on my CL Card?

You can deposit crypto on your CL Card through Ledger Live. Navigate to the Card section and transfer funds from your Ledger wallet.

I haven't made any purchases, so why has my balance decreased?

Your total balance is tied to the value of the crypto you hold. This value, and your balance, will increase or decrease depending on the market conditions.

Managing Stableloan*** 

How does a Stableloan work?

You can enter the terms of the Stableloan you want to use with the calculator—for example, the amount, term length and interest rate—and it will tell you how much crypto you need to deposit on your CL account.

CL Card will hold your crypto as security (or collateral) against the Stableloan. You will receive the full amount of deposited crypto back in your wallet as soon as you settle the Stableloan and interest payments.

What crypto can I deposit as collateral?

Currently, you can deposit BTC and ETH as collateral.

Can I receive a Stableloan in different currencies?

At the moment, you can receive Stableloan in USDC, USDT or EURT. 

If I take a Stableloan, how long can I keep it?

You can keep it for 3, 6, 12 or 24 months.
You can settle the Stableloan at any time and receive collateral back in your wallet. Just keep in mind that you need to pay the full principal amount and all interest.

How do I pay interest on a Stableloan?

Every month, your wallet will be credited with the loan interest payment. If your wallet doesn’t have sufficient balance to cover interest, you can make the payment later in the Stableloans section of the Ledger Live app. Any Stableloans with late or missing interest payments will be highlighted. If you miss multiple monthly interest payments, the CL team will inform you about payment schedules and penalties.

Why do I need to add more collateral to my Stableloan?

In case there is the risk of exceeding deposited collateral or if your LTV exceeds the agreed amount, the CL team will contact you to add further collateral value and reduce the LTV.

What is LTV?

LTV stands for loan-to-value and refers to the ratio of a loan compared to the value of an asset provided as collateral.
Loan → principal amount that a borrower receives.
Value → the value of the collateral asset that a lender receives.

How do I add more collateral to my Stableloan?

If you have sufficient funds in your wallet, you can add collateral to your Stableloan in the Ledger Live app. 

Do you allow early settlements of Stableloans?

Yes, you can settle your Stableloan and unlock collateral at any point during or beyond the Stableloan term.

Can I have multiple Stableloans active at the same time?

Yes. There is no limit to how many active Stableloans you can have at the same time.

Managing Cryptodraft***

What is Cryptodraft?

With Cryptodraft, you can spend up to 60% of your collateral value as a credit.

What interest can I earn with Cryptodraft?

Spending the first 10% is interest-free, while the remaining 50% is subject to a 1% monthly-percentage-rate (MPR). The monthly-percentage-rate represents the nominal interest rate accumulated on the principal draft amount each month.

Can I fully repay my Cryptodraft?

You can. Select Settle → Pay Now. This allows you to repay your full Cryptodraft principal amount and any due interest.

Can I partially repay my Cryptodraft?

You can. Select Settle → Pay Partial. This allows you to repay any amount you wish—there is no minimum.

What is the maximum amount I can deposit at once?

The maximum amount you can deposit at once is 2.5 BTC or 32 ETH.

What can I use as collateral?

You can use BTC or ETH.
