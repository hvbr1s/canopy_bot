I can't see my USDT deposit in my Ledger Tron account

This article covers the most frequent reason when you aren't able to see your USDT deposit in your Tron (TRX) account in Ledger Live.

USDT is the TRC20 USD-pegged stablecoin issued by Tether on the TRON network. In order to receive TRC20 tokens, you have to use your Tron receiving address from Ledger Live. Learn more.

I sent my USDT to Ledger Tron address and still can't see it in Ledger Live

For the newly created accounts on Tron, you need first to activate them in order to see your USDT or any other TRC10 / TRC20 tokens.

The minimum required amount for the account to be activated is 0.1 TRX. Learn more.

So if you did send USDT to Ledger Tron account and if they are not visible, please make sure you have at least 0.1 TRX on the main Tron account where you sent USDT. Only then your USDT will appear in the Tron parent account.
