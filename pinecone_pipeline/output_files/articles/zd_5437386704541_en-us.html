Osmosis (OSMO)

This article will help you set up your first Ledger Osmosis (OSMO) account.

 Important note

Please ensure your Ledger Live app is updated to version 2.62.2 (for desktop) or 3.26.0 (for mobile) or higher. Using outdated versions could lead to synchronization issues and failed transactions. You can find the latest update for Ledger Live here.

Temporary network issues with Osmosis

  • OSMO users may encounter Cannot read properties errors while attempting to manage or stake OSMO through Ledger Live.



  • If you are experiencing these issues, we recommend closing Ledger Live and using Keplr Wallet with your Ledger, as a temporary solution. Our team is actively working on resolving this matter

Before you start

  • Ensure Ledger Live is updated to version 2.62.2 (for desktop) or 3.26.0 (for mobile) or higher. Using outdated versions could lead to synchronization issues and failed transactions. You can find the latest update for Ledger Live here.
  • Update the firmware of your Ledger Nano S / Nano S Plus / Nano X.

Install the Cosmos (ATOM) app on your Ledger device

  1. Open Ledger Live and navigate to the My Ledger tab.
  2. Connect and unlock your Ledger device.
  3. If asked, allow My Ledger on your device.
  4. Search for the Cosmos (ATOM) app in the app catalog.
  5. Click the Install button to install the app on your Ledger device.
    • Your Ledger device displays Processing.
    • Ledger Live displays Installed.

Add your Osmosis account in Ledger Live

  1. Open Ledger Live and navigate to the Accounts tab.
  2. Click the Add account button.
  3. Type or click the drop-down list to select Osmosis. Then click Continue.
  4. Connect and unlock your device. Then open the Cosmos app on your device.
    Ledger Live will look for existing accounts in the blockchain. These are then displayed one by one.
  5. In the Accounts step, different sections can appear:
    • In the Add existing accounts section, tick the existing account you want to add.
    • In the Add new account section, tick to add a new account.
    • The Accounts already in Portfolio section lists the accounts that are already in Portfolio and thus can not be added.
  6. Click Add account

You have successfully added an Osmosis account to the Portfolio.
