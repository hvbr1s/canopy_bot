How to check your Solana (SOL) staking rewards via Solana Beach

This article describes how to use Solana Beach to obtain a breakdown of your Solana (SOL) staking rewards.* For more information, check the Solana wallet page. 

 Please note that SOL staking rewards won't show up as transactions in your Ledger Live's Transaction history. Instead, these rewards will directly increase your Delegated assets balance. For instructions on viewing the reward breakdown with Solana beach explorer, continue reading.

Before you start

  • Make sure to update Ledger Live to the latest version via this link.
  • Open the Solana app on your Ledger device.

Step-by-step

  1. Open Ledger Live and navigate to the Accounts tab.
  2. In your account, click Receive to obtain your Solana account address.



  3. Go to solanabeach.io
  4. In the block explorer paste your Solana address into the search bar and press enter.
    The explorer displays your Account Details.
  5. Navigate to the Stakes tab and select a staking account.
    The explorer displays your Stake Account Details.
  6. Navigate to the Stake Rewards tab.
    The breakdown of your staking rewards for this account appears.
     You can also download a .csv file of all the reward transactions directly via the explorer.

 

*Rewards are not guaranteed. Ledger does not provide any financial advice or recommendation.
