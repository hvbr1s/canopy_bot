PulseChain (PLS)

This article describes how to create your first PulseChain (PLS) account with your Ledger device. Visit the PulseChain website to learn more about the project.
Learn more about how to manage crypto assets with your Ledger device here.

Before you start

  • Download and install the latest version of Ledger live via this link.
  • Make sure your Ledger device runs the latest firmware. Learn more.

Video instructions

Install the Ethereum (ETH) app on your Ledger device

  1. Open My Ledger in Ledger Live.
  2. Connect and unlock your Ledger device.
  3. If asked, allow My Ledger to access your device.
  4. Search for Ethereum (ETH) in the app catalog.
  5. Click Install. 

Connect your Ledger to PulseChain network

  1. Close Ledger Live.
  2. Follow this tutorial to connect your Ledger to Metamask.



  3. Follow this tutorial to connect MetaMask to PulseChain network with ChainList.



  4. Copy your PulseChain address to send PLS coins to your Ledger wallet.



    You're all done!
