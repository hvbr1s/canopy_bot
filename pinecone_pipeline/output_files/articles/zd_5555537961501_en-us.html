OKXChain (OKT)

Install the OKXChain app on your Ledger device to manage OKT coins with MetaMask. The app is developed and supported by the OKX community. 

 OKT Token accounts cannot be added to Ledger Live. Learn more about how to manage your crypto assets with your Ledger device here.

Before you start

  • Set up Ledger Live with your device.
  • Update the firmware of your Ledger Nano S Plus / Nano X.

Install the OKXChain app

  1. Open My Ledger in Ledger Live.
  2. Connect and unlock your Ledger device.
  3. If asked, allow My Ledger on your device.
  4. Find OKXChain in the app catalog.
  5. Click the Install button of the app.
    • Your Ledger device will display Processing... 
    • The app installation is confirmed.

Next steps

  • Create your first Ledger OKXChain account via Metamask. More on this here.
  • Reach out to the OKXChain support team on Discord if you require assistance.
