[ Ledger ] Market FAQ

[ Ledger ] Market is a platform for curated NFT drops that enables artists and brands to create, distribute, and store NFTs with ease and world-class security. 

Through curation, artist-in-residence programs, custom collaborations, and product drops, [ Ledger ] Market gives you true ownership and control at every stage of the NFT experience, from minting to safeguarding. 

 To find more information about  [ Ledger ] Market, please visit our official website.


 How to mint a [ Ledger ] Market Genesis Pass NFT with Ledger Live

  • On July 25, 2022 at 6pm CET, selected accounts will be able to mint a limited-edition Genesis Pass NFT to celebrate the launch of [ Ledger ] Market.
  • You can find more information about how to securely mint a Genesis Pass via Ledger Live here.
  • Minting via Ledger Live is the most secure option but, alternatively, you can also mint your NFT via Metamask, WalletConnect and Coinbase Wallet.
