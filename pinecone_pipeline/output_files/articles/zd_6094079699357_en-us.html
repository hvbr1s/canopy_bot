Secret (SCRT)

This article will help you create your first Secret (SCRT) account with your Ledger and the Ping.pub GUI wallet. The Secret app is developed and supported by the Secret Network community. 

 Please note that Secret accounts are not currently supported in Ledger Live.

Before you start

  • Set up Ledger Live with your device.
  • Update the firmware of your Ledger Nano S / Nano S Plus / Nano X.

Install the Secret app

  1. Open Ledger Live and navigate to the My Ledger tab.
  2. Connect and unlock your Ledger device.
  3. If asked, allow My Ledger on your device.
  4. Search for the Secret (SCRT) app in the app catalog.
  5. Click the Install button to install the app on your Ledger device.
    • Your Ledger device displays Processing.
    • Ledger Live displays Installed.

Next steps

  • Create your first Ledger Secret account. Check the Secret user guide for setup instructions.
  • Reach out to the Secret support team on their support page if you require assistance.
