How to Join the Refer a Friend program

Refer a friend is Ledger’s referral program that lets you invite friends to buy Ledger Nano wallets and get $10 in Bitcoin once they complete the purchase.

To participate, you need to sign in with your ETH account. The steps are as follows:

  1. Open the Ledger Live app. 
  2. In the left side menu, click Refer a friend. 
  3. Connect your Ledger device with a computer and enter the PIN.
  4. Click Sign in with your Ethereum account.

  1. Select the Ethereum address you want to sign in with.
  2. Click Connect.

  1. Open the Ethereum app on your Ledger device.
  2. Review and sign the message on your device.

Now you can access your referral dashboard. There you will find your referral link. Copy the link and share it with your friends.

Purchased a Ledger device using the referral link? Redeem your $10 in Bitcoin:

  1. Once you've received your Ledger device, scratch the card that comes with it to reveal the redemption code. 
  2. In the Redeem Gift Code tab, click Redeem my gift code.

  1. Sign in with your ETH account.
  2. Enter your gift code. 

Once your code is validated, your crypto gift will be available to claim in 30 days.

Claim your reward

30 days after receiving your reward, you can claim it. Please note that you have 1 year to claim your reward. The reward will be displayed in your balance. Referral rewards are in BTC. To receive them, you need to have a BTC address. 

  1. Click Claim.
  2. Add your new or existing Bitcoin account where the reward will be sent. 
  3. Follow the on-screen instructions to finalize the process.
