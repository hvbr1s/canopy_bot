How to register your Ethereum account for the BRICK mint

This article describes how to register your Ledger Ethereum account for the BRICK mint.
 You can learn more about the Ledger x BRICK mint here.

  • You can check whether your account has been selected here.

 I hold multiple Genesis Pass NFTs in my account, will I be able to mint multiple BRICK NFTs?

  • No, a registered account can only mint once.
  • You can, however, create multiple Ledger Ethereum accounts, distribute your Passes across these accounts and register each one by following the steps below.
  • More questions about the mint are answered in our official Ledger x BRICK FAQ here.

Before you start

  • Make sure to update Ledger Live to the latest version via this link.
  • Open Ledger Live and connect your Ledger device to My Ledger to ensure that your Ledger device is running the latest firmware version. If not, please update.
  • Ensure that the latest version of the Ethereum (ETH) app is installed on your Ledger device.
  • You'll need a Twitter account.
  • You'll need at least 0.1337 ETH in your Ledger Ethereum (ETH) account to register.

 Step-by-step

    1. Connect your Ledger device to your computer and open the Ethereum (ETH) app on your device.
      Your Ledger device displays: Application is ready.
    2. Connect your Ledger Ethereum (ETH) account to Metamask by following these steps.
    3. Go to the [ L ] Market website by clicking this link.
    4. On the website, select Join Genesis Pass Allowlist.
      You'll be automatically redirected to the Premint website.

      Not a Genesis Pass holder?

      • No problems, you can select Join Allowlist Raffle here for a chance to win a mint spot on the allowlist.
      • Genesis Pass NFTs are also available to purchase on the secondary market. More on this here.
      • Registered Genesis Pass holders are guaranteed a mint spot.
    5. On the Premint website, select Login to Register, Connect Wallet then select Metamask.
      Metamask opens in your browser.
    6. After making sure the Ethereum app is open on your Ledger, click Sign.
      Your Ledger displays: Sign message.
    7. Using the buttons, scroll right, reading the message as you go. If all looks good, select Sign message and confirm by pressing both buttons simultaneously.
    8. Back to Premint, navigate to the Twitter section and click Connect.
    9. Once done, go to BRICK's official Twitter account and click the Follow button.
    10. Select Click to Register to finalize the registration.
      Premint displays: Registered.

      You're done. Please follow Ledger's official Twitter account for updates about the mint.
