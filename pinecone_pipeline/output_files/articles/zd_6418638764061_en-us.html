How to access your Cardano NFTs via JPG.Store and Nami

This article describes how to access your Cardano (ADA) NFTs via Jpg.Store and the Nami app.
  You can learn more about jpg.store here.

Check the Cardano wallet page to learn more. 

Before you start

  • Update Ledger Live to the latest version via this link.
  • Open Ledger Live and connect your Ledger device to the My Ledger to ensure that your Ledger device is running the latest firmware version.
  • Ensure that the latest version of the Cardano app is installed on your Ledger device.

Step-by-step:

  1. Connect your Ledger to Nami by following these steps.
  2. Go to the jpg.store website.
  3. On the website, click Connect Wallet.
  4. Select Nami.
    Your ADA balance appears.
  5. Hover your mouse over your ADA balance and select Profile > My NFTs.
    Your NFT collection appears.
