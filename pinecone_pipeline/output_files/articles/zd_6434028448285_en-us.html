How to manage your Cardano NFTs via JPG.Store and Eternl

This article describes how to manage and sell your Cardano (ADA) NFTs via Jpg.Store and the Eternl app. Check the Cardano wallet page to learn more. 
  You can learn more about jpg.store here.

Before you start

  • Update Ledger Live to the latest version via this link.
  • Open Ledger Live and connect your Ledger device to My Ledger to ensure that your Ledger device is running the latest firmware version.
  • Ensure that the latest version of the Cardano (ADA) app is installed on your Ledger device.

Access your Cardano NFTs

  1. Connect your Ledger to Eternl by following these steps.
  2. In Eternl, select Dapp Browser > jpg.store then click Connect As DApp Account.
  3. Go to jpg.store
  4. Click Connect Wallet and select Eternl.
    Eternl pops up on your screen.
  5. Select Connect To Site.
    Your ADA balance appears in jpg.store.
  6. Click on your ADA balance > Profile > My NFTs
    Your NFT collection appears.

Sell a Cardano NFT

  1. In your Profile and select the NFT you wish to sell.
  2. Choose a selling price then click Send listing.
    Eternl pops up on your screen.
  3. Click Sign.
    Your Ledger displays: New Plutus transaction?
  4. Using the buttons on your Ledger, review the transaction and, if all looks good sign the transaction.

    Your Ledger displays: Cardano is ready.
    Jpg.store displays: Your transaction is being processed.

    Once the Cardano network has confirmed your transaction, your NFT will be listed for sale and displayed in your Profile under the For Sale section.
