Activate Tron account to send or receive TRC20 tokens

When you interact with a new Tron account in Ledger Live, one of the following messages may appear:

  • You need to activate your Tron account before depositing TRC20 tokens
  • You first need to send at least 0.1 TRX to this address to enable TRC20 deposit

These messages appear due to the rules of the Tron protocol that requires you to activate your Tron account before receiving any TRC20 tokens. To activate your Tron account, you must send at least 0.1 TRX.
