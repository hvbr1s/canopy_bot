Change language settings on your Ledger device

All Ledger devices are available by default in English. The latest firmware versions of Ledger Nano S Plus and Ledger Nano X feature new device languages: French and Spanish. Support for more languages is expected to be released. 

The features of the operating system such as device settings, control center, battery indications, etc. can now be displayed in your preferred language. However, Nano apps such as the Bitcoin or Ethereum app are only available in English for the moment.

 Note that this feature is not supported by Ledger Nano S.

Before you start

  • Make sure you have updated Ledger Live through the notification banner or downloaded the latest version of Ledger Live. 
  • Make sure your Ledger device is running the latest firmware version:
    • Ledger Nano S Plus: Ensure that your device is updated to firmware version 1.1.0 or above. Learn how to update your Ledger Nano S Plus here.
    • Ledger Nano X: Ensure that your device is updated to firmware version 2.1.0 or above. Learn how to update your Ledger Nano X here.

Instructions

  Ledger Live Desktop   Ledger Live Mobile
  1. In Ledger Live, go to My Ledger. 
  2. Connect and unlock your Ledger device. If asked, allow the manager on your device.
  3. In My Ledger, click on the language currently used on your device.
    A Device Language right-pane appears.

  4. Select the language that you want to set on your device.

  5. Press both buttons on your device to allow language installation.  
  6. Wait for the Installing Language loader to reach 100%.
  7. Click Close. 

You have successfully changed the language on your device. 

  1. Open the Ledger Live app on your smartphone. 
  2. (only for Ledger Nano S Plus users) Connect your Ledger device to your smartphone using a USB-C cable. 
  3. Tap the My Ledger tab. 
  4. Select the device for which you want to change the device language. 
  5. Tap on the language currently used on your Ledger device.
    The Device Language page appears.

  6. Select the language you want to install on your Ledger device.

  7. Tap the Change language button.  
  8. Press both buttons on your Ledger device to allow language installation.  
  9. Wait for the Installing Language loader to reach 100%.
  10. Tap Continue. 

You have successfully changed the language on your device.
