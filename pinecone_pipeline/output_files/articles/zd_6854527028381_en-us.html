Acala (ACA)

This tutorial will show you how to create your first Acala (ACA) account with your Ledger device and polkadot.js.

 'Txn version not supported' error

This error signifies that your Alcala (ACA) app is currently out of date. To continue using your account as usual, please connect your Ledger device to MyLedger via Ledger Live, and proceed to update the Alcala (ACA) app to the latest version. 

Before you start

  • Ensure that you're running the latest version of Ledger Live. 
  • Ensure that your Ledger device is running the latest firmware version.

Install the Acala (ACA) on your Ledger device through Ledger Live

  1. Open Ledger Live and navigate to the My Ledger  tab.
  2. Connect and unlock your Ledger device.
  3. If asked, allow My Ledger on your device.
  4. Search for the Acala app in the app catalog.
  5. Click the Install button of the app.
    After a short moment, the word Installed appears in Ledger Live, confirming the installation is successful.


 The Acala (ACA) app is developed and supported by the Acala community.

Create your Acala (ACA) account with your Ledger and polkadot.js

  1. Connect your Ledger device to your computer and unlock it.
  2. Open the Acala app.
    Your Ledger displays: Acala Ready.
  3. Go to polkadot.js.org
  4. Click Settings.
  5. Click Acala -> via Acala Foundation 1.
  6. Click Switch.
  7. Go to Settings.
  8. Select Attach Ledger via WebHID and click Save.
  9. Navigate to Accounts -> Add via Ledger.
  10. Name your account.
  11. Leave the account type and index values at 0 and click + Save.

    Your Ledger Acala account appears in the app. You can now use this account to send and receive ACA.
