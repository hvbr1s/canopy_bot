Polkadex (PDEX)

This tutorial will show you how to create your first Ledger Polkadex (PDEX) account with your Ledger device and polkadot.js.
 The Polkadex app is developed and supported by the Polkadex community.

Before you start

  • Update Ledger Live through the notification banner or download the latest version.
  • Update the firmware on your Ledger Nano S / Nano S Plus / Nano X.

All set? Great, let’s dive in.

Install the Polkadex (PDEX) app on your Ledger device

  1. Connect and unlock your Ledger device.
  2. Open Ledger Live and navigate to My Ledger.
  3. If asked, allow access to My Ledger by pressing both buttons on your Ledger device simultaneously. 
  4. In My Ledger, select the App catalog, search for Polkadex (PDEX) and click Install.
    You're done.
