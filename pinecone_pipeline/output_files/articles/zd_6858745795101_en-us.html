Zerion App - Ledger Live

This tutorial will show you how to operate the Zerion app with your Ledger device through the Discover section in Ledger Live. 

What is Zerion?

Zerion is an investing platform connecting you to everything Web3 has to offer, including NFTs and all things crypto. They offer mobile connectivity so you can start investing and trading crypto assets immediately and on the go. 

 Important Notice for Zerion Users

You might be surprised to learn that using Zerion requires the download of the Paraswap app. While you might anticipate needing the ETH app or a dedicated Zerion app, please note that Paraswap is essential for accessing Zerion's services. Please make sure the Paraswap app is open on your Ledger device before interacting with Zerion.

Where can I find Zerion in Ledger Live?

Located within the Discover section of Ledger Live, this is a Ledger-approved application that can help users do various operations with cryptocurrencies.

Before you begin

  • Update Ledger Live through the notification banner or download the latest version.
  • Update the firmware of your Ledger Nano S / Nano S Plus / Nano X.

Zerion interface overview

This app interface consists of five main elements: 

  1. Wallet selection: Select the account you want to interact with.
  2. Advanced settings: Choose your gas, slippage, load crypto, and toggle discreet mode.
  3. In-app Menu: Initiate swaps, choose favorites, and general Zerion settings.
  4. Overview/dashboard: View the history of token/NFT transactions through a price graph.
  5. Networks: Seamlessly toggle between various crypto networks. 

How to swap with Zerion in Ledger Live 

  Ledger Live Desktop   Ledger Live Mobile - Android only
  1. In Ledger Live, click Discover on the left panel.
  2. Select the Zerion app.
  3. In the in-app menu, click swap. 
  4. Enter the amount you want to swap.
  5. If everything looks good, approve the transaction.
  6. Sign the transaction (quickly press both buttons) on your Ledger device if everything is correct.

  1. In Ledger Live mobile, click the Discover button.
  2. Select click Earn.
  3. Select the Zerion app.
  4. Click Swap.
  5. Enter the amount you want to swap.
  6. If everything looks good, approve the transaction.
  7. Sign the transaction (quickly press both buttons) on your Ledger device if everything is correct.


Features of Zerion

Swapping/Bridging

Zerion prides itself in having seamless swapping and bridging options. It also has full NFT portfolio functionalities such as trading and tracking.

New browser extension/dApp browser - coming soon
The new browser allows you to have access to thousands of dApps, natively, within the Zerion interface. More information coming soon! 
Explore Section
The explore section is unique as well. It has a full array of various trending Defi projects, current ETH merge POS yields, and various indexes with the goal of making web3 a consistent, pleasurable experience. 
Portfolio Tracking

Portfolio tracking allows you visually track your Defi portfolio with ease. The app gives you a clear picture of the interest you've earned by investing in things like liquidity pools or providing collateral to crypto loans. It also gives you full visibility to borrow crypto and leverage the market, or trade tokens at competitive rates.
