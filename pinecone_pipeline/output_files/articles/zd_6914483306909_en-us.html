Karura (KAR)

Install the Karura app on your Ledger device to manage KAR with the Polkadot web wallet. The Karura app is developed and supported by the Karura community. 

 Karura accounts cannot be added to Ledger Live.

Before you start

  • Download and install Ledger Live on your computer. You can learn more about how to get started with Ledger Live here.
  • Open Ledger Live, connect your Ledger device to My Ledger and make sure your Ledger device is running the latest firmware version.
  • Update the firmware of your Ledger Nano S / Nano S Plus / Nano X.

All set? Great, let’s dive in.

Install the Karura (KAR) app on your Ledger device

  1. Connect and unlock your Ledger device.
  2. Open Ledger Live and navigate to My Ledger.
  3. If asked, allow access to My Ledger by pressing both buttons on your Ledger device simultaneously. 
  4. In My Ledger, select the App catalog, search for Karura (KAR), and click Install.
    You're done.
