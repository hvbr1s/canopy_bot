Bitrefill

This tutorial will show you how to use the Bitrefill app with your Ledger device through the Discover section in Ledger Live. 

What is Bitrefill?

Bitrefill is a platform that offers services and products so that people can use cryptocurrencies more easily. This service provides a menu of prepaid gift cards and mobile phone recharges. 

For more information about Bitrefill, please view the official website.

How does Bitrefill work in Ledger Live?

Located within the Discover section of Ledger Live, this is a Ledger-approved application that can seamlessly help users to get services and products in exchange for their crypto, from their accounts.

Before you begin

  • Update Ledger Live through the notification banner or download the latest version.
  • Ensure your Ledger device runs the latest firmware version.
  • Make sure the proper apps (eg. BTC, ETH, DOGE) are installed and updated on your device.
  • Make sure you have funds in the accounts to pay for the services and gift cards.

How to use Bitrefill

  Ledger Live Desktop   Ledger Live Mobile
  1. In Ledger Live, click Discover on the left panel.
  2. Select the Bitrefill app.
  3. Select service or gift card (eg. Amazon).
  4. Enter the amount of the gift card (eg. 20$).
  5. Click Add to cart.
  6. Click Checkout on the cart icon.
  7. Enter your email address for order status updates.
  8. Read and tick the box with Terms and Conditions and the Privacy Policy.
  9. Click Continue.
  10. Choose Payment method (BTC, ETH, DOGE, LTC).
  11. Verify order details and click Pay if everything is correct.
    The sign transaction window appears.
  12. Check the information and click Continue.
    Make sure your Ledger device is connected to your PC and unlocked.
  13. Verify and sign the transaction on your Ledger device.

That's it. Once the transaction is confirmed, Bitrefill will send to your email address a gift card/code that you can use.

  10. Click Continue.
