SORA (XOR) x Ledger upcoming integration

This article provides updated information about our upcoming integration with SORA (XOR). 
 You can learn more about SORA here.

What's SORA? 

According to the SORA team, SORA is "an on-chain governance system based around the XOR token that allows producers to make proposals for funding and all token holders make decisions how to allocate XOR so that the best goods and services get created. People who create goods or services should accept XOR for them, thus building the SORA economy".

On February 6, 2022, SORA won the auction to become a Kusama parachain. You can find more details here.

Technical documentation about SORA is available here.

When is the integration happening? 

While we can't pinpoint a specific timeframe, it should be soon and updates will be posted here and on the Ledger Support Twitter account.

Once SORA is supported by Ledger, you will be able to download the SORA app on your Ledger via Ledger Live and use your device to create a Ledger SORA account via a third-party app like polkadot.js. 

What should I do for now?

Hang tight! You can follow us on Twitter for updates.
