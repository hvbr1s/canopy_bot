Invalid Address error when sending XDC coins

This article describes how to solve the Invalid Address error when sending XDC coins from MyEtherWallet.



This issue also affects MetaMask in the form of a Not ETH network error.



Solution

Both these errors can be solved by replacing the prefix "xdc" with "0x" in the destination address.

For example:
xdc3d49D8A92274C5C7be99B331E05f3851eB51D234

becomes

0x3d49D8A92274C5C7be99B331E05f3851eB51D234



This will allow MyEtherWallet and MetaMask to understand the destination address and send your coins on the XDC Network to the correct account without any issues.

If this is the first time you're running into this issue, we strongly recommend sending a small test XDC amount first.
