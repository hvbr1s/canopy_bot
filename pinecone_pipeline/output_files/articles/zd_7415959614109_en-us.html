Kadena (KDA)

This tutorial will show you how to create your first Ledger Kadena (KDA) account with your Ledger device connected to a tool called Kadena Transfer.

  Kadena accounts are not supported in Ledger Live at the moment.

Before you start

  • Ensure you're running the latest version of Ledger Live or download the latest version.
  • Update the firmware on your Ledger Nano S / Nano S Plus / Nano X.

All set? Great, let’s dive in.

Video instructions


Install the Kadena (KDA) app on your Ledger device

  1. Connect and unlock your Ledger device.
  2. Open Ledger Live and navigate to My Ledger.
  3. If asked, allow access to My Ledger by pressing both buttons on your Ledger device simultaneously. 
  4. In My Ledger, select the App catalog, search for Kadena (KDA) and click Install.
    You're done.



     The Kadena app is developed and supported by the Kadena community.

Connect your Ledger device to Kadena Transfer app

  1. Go to the Kadena Transfer website.
  2. Open the Kadena app on your Ledger device, the word Kadena should be displayed on your Ledger screen.



  3. On the website, click Show Ledger Account Name. From the pop-up menu, select your Ledger device and click Connect.



  4. Your Kadena (KDA) account address will be displayed within the app. To ensure this address is secure, it's crucial to verify it on your device. Click Verify and Provide Public Key should appear on your Ledger's screen.



  5. Carefully check that the address on your Ledger device matches the one shown in the app. If they match, move to the Confirm screen on your Ledger device and press both buttons simultaneously to validate the address.



    Done! You can now copy the verified address and proceed to send Kadena (KDA) coins to your Ledger wallet
 Always include the k: prefix in your Kadena (KDA) address. Omitting it may result in lost funds. If you're sending KDA coins to your Ledger wallet for the first time, always start with a small test amount.
