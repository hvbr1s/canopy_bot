NFT features disabled for iOS

This article describes the changes made to the Ledger Live iOS app after Apple updated its Apple Store guidelines regarding NFTs.
 You can learn more about Apple's new NFT guidelines here.

A bit of background 

On October 24, 2022, Apple released an updated version of its App Store guidelines. This version includes restrictive requirements regarding how NFTs may be managed in apps listed on the App Store.

As a result, starting from v3.11, the following features will no longer be supported on the Ledger Live iOS app:

  • Sending NFTs
  • External links to [ Ledger ] Market website
  • External links to Rarible and OpenSea
  • Floor price

You can send NFTs via the Ledger Live desktop app. More on this here.

You can access [ Ledger ] Market via official website or via the Ledger Live desktop app. More on this here.
