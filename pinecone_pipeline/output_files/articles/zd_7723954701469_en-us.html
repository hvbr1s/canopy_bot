How to access your Solana tokens

This article describes how to access Solana tokens stored in your Ledger Solana account. Check the Solana wallet page to learn more. 

I can't see my Solana tokens in Ledger Live

At present, Ledger Live does not provide support for Solana tokens. However, you can securely access and manage your tokens by pairing your Ledger Solana account with Solflare.

Before you start

  • Make sure the latest version of the Solana (SOL) app is installed on your Ledger device.
  • Make sure to close Ledger Live as it can conflict with Solflare.

Step 1 - Connect your Ledger Solana account to Solflare

  1. Go to Solflare.
  2. Connect your Ledger device and open the Solana app.
    Application is ready appears on the screen.



  3. Click Access Wallet.



  4. Select Ledger > Continue.



  5. Click Continue then select your Ledger device and click Connect.

  6. Choose and confirm a password then click Continue.



  7. Select m/44'/501'/0' for the derivation path.



  8. Select your account then click Continue.



    Your Ledger Solana (SOL) account appears.



Step 2 - Send a token

  1. On your Ledger device, open the Solana app then go to Settings > Allow blind signing and press both buttons to select Yes. You can find more detailed instructions here.
  2. Select a token and click Send.



  3. Select the amount and the recipient address then click Send.



    Your Ledger device displays: Transfer tokens...
  4. Use the buttons on your Ledger to review the transaction. If all looks good, navigate to Approve and press both buttons simultaneously to sign the transaction.



    You're done. Once the transaction is confirmed on the Solana blockchain, the token balance will appear in the destination address.

My token is not displayed in Solflare

In case the token is not automatically added to Solflare, you may have to add it manually. To do this, simply click on the '+ Add New Asset' option in Solflare and enter the token ticker by typing.
