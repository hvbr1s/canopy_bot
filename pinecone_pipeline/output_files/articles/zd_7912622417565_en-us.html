How to connect your Ledger Litecoin (LTC) account to Electrum

This article describes how to connect your Ledger Litecoin (LTC) account to Electrum.

Check the Litecoin wallet page to learn more.

Before you start

  • Make sure the latest version of the Litecoin (LTC) app is installed on your Ledger device.

  • Make sure to close Ledger Live as it can conflict with Electrum.

Connect your Litecoin (LTC) account to Electrum

  1. Go to electrum-ltc.org, select Download and install Electrum on your computer.
  2. On your computer, open Electrum. If asked how you want to connect to a server, select Auto connectÂ and click Next.
  3. Name your wallet (or keep the default name) and click Next.
  4. Choose a Standard wallet and click Next.
  5. On your Ledger device, check that the Litecoin app is open.
    Your Ledger displays: Application is ready.
  6. Back to Electrum, select Use a hardware device and click Next.
  7. Select your device and click Next.



  8. Select your account derivation path (which by default should be native segwit) then click Next.



    If you're unsure which derivation path to select, check your account derivation path in Ledger Live then close Ledger Live.



  9. If asked, select Encrypt wallet file and click Next.



    Your Ledger Litecoin (LTC) account and transaction history appears.
    You're done!

    If not, please go back to step 8 and choose a different derivation path.
