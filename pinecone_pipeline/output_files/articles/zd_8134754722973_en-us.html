Connect your Ledger to Tron with TronLink

This tutorial describes how to connect your Ledger Tron account to TronLink.
  Learn more about TronLink here. Check the Tron wallet page to learn more. 

Before you start

  • Make sure that the latest version of the Tron app is installed on your Ledger device.
  • Close the Ledger Live app.

Connect your Ledger Tron account to TronLink

  1. Connect your Ledger device to your computer, unlock it and open the Tron app.
    Your Ledger displays: Application is ready.
  2. Go to the TronLink website and download the extension on your browser.
  3. Open the extension and select pair Ledger hardware wallet.

  4. Accept the user agreement.
  5. Click Done. Next.

  6. Name your account, choose and confirm a password then click Confirm to finish importing your Ledger account.


    Your Ledger Tron account will appear. You're all set!
