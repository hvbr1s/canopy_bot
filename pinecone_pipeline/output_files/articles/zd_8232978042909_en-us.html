What is Constellation? 

Constellation is a crypto project built on top of the Hypergraph network. Instead of a traditional blockchain structure, Constellation uses a Directed Acyclic Graph or DAG for storing data and tracking ownership of its native asset (also called DAG). 

Technical documentation about the Constellation project is available here.

Does Ledger support Constellation? 

No, Ledger currently doesn't support Constellation and we're not yet able to provide an ETA regarding the integration. Learn more about how to manage crypto assets with your Ledger device here.
