How to redeem Ledger Stax NFT and claim your Ledger Stax?

Here is a step-by-step guide on redeeming your Ledger Stax NFT to claim a Ledger Stax.

Before you start

  • Ensure that you're running the latest version of Ledger Live.
  • Ensure that your Ledger device is running the latest firmware version.
  • Ensure that the latest version of the Ethereum (ETH) app is installed on your Ledger device.
  • Blind signing should be enabled in the Ethereum (ETH) app.
  • Once you're all done, close Ledger Live as it can conflict with MetaMask.

Redeem Ledger Stax NFT

Go to ➡️ https://shop.ledger.com/products/ledger-stax-redeem and click Claim.

You’ll be asked to connect your wallet so that it can be scanned for an eligible Ledger Stax Redemption Token.

When you use desktop, you have two options to connect a wallet:

    • MetaMask
    • WalletConnect, which allows you to access a range of wallets, including Ledger Live, Crypto.com, Zerion, Tokenary, and more.

On mobile, you can only use WalletConnect. But don’t worry, with WalletConnect you can access a number of wallets, including MetaMask. 

 

Claim Ledger Stax with MetaMask (desktop users)
  1. Download the MetaMask extension and connect your Ledger Ethereum (ETH) account. Learn more
  2. Back on the claim page, select MetaMask. The browser extension will open and you’ll see your Ethereum account and balance. 



  3. Ensure your Ledger device is unlocked and that the Ethereum (ETH) app is opened on your device. You’ll need it to securely sign the transaction and redeem your token.
  4. Click Sign to initiate the transaction.



    Your Ledger device displays: Sign message
  5. Using the Ledger device buttons, review the message that displays on the screen.
    If all looks good, navigate to Sign message and press both buttons simultaneously to confirm.
  6. You should see a message stating Success. 
  7. Select your Ledger Stax Redemption Token from the list of available tokens. 
  8. Click Checkout now. You’ll be taken to the checkout page. A discount code will be automatically applied to your cart.
    Keep in mind that once you start the checkout process, your NFT will be marked as redeemed. If the checkout process is interrupted, you'll have to connect your wallet again to resume it. 
  9. Fill out your order details and click Continue.
  10. Pick the shipping method:
    • You can opt for standard shipping with Colissimo that is free, or
    • Choose express shipping with DHL which includes additional fees depending on the country.

      Click Continue. 

      Accept the terms and conditions and click Complete Order. You’ll see the notification that your order is confirmed and you will receive a confirmation email shortly after.

      That’s it. You're done!
Claim Ledger Stax with WalletConnect (mobile users)
  1. Make sure your Ledger is unlocked and connected to your mobile via USB (Nano S Plus) or Bluetooth (Nano X). You’ll need it to sign the transaction and redeem your token.
  2. Click Wallet Connect.

  3. You’ll be presented with the option to connect your wallet: via scanning a QR code or via Mobile.
  4. Select Mobile -> Ledger.
  5. Select Open Ledger Live.
  6. Click Sign to process the transaction. You should see a message stating Success. 
  7. Select your Redemption Token from the list of available tokens. 
  8. Click Checkout now. You’ll be taken to the checkout page. A discount code will be automatically applied to your cart. Keep in mind that you have 10 minutes to complete the purchase once you start the checkout process. 
  9. Fill out the customer information—your email address, shipping address, and phone number—and click Continue. Your shipping address will not be associated with your Ethereum account. 
  10. Pick the shipping method:
    • You can opt for standard shipping with Colissimo that is free, or
    • Choose express shipping with DHL which includes additional fees depending on the country.

      Click Continue. 

      Accept the terms and conditions and click Complete Order. You’ll see the notification that your order is confirmed and you will receive a confirmation email shortly after.

      That’s it. You're done!
