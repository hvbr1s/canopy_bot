How to claim your Ledgerverse NFTs in Sandbox?

Here is a step-by-step guide on claiming your Ledgerverse NFTs in Sandbox.
 Click here to learn more about Ledgerverse.

Before you start

  • Ensure that you're running the latest version of Ledger Live.
  • Ensure that your Ledger device is running the latest firmware version.
  • Ensure that the latest version of the Ethereum (ETH) app is installed on your Ledger device.
  • Blind signing should be enabled in the Ethereum (ETH) app.
  • Once you're all done, close Ledger Live as it can conflict with MetaMask.

Claim your Ledgerverse NFTs in Sandbox

  1. Open MetaMask and connect your Ledger Ethereum (ETH) account. Learn more
  2. Go to https://www.sandbox.game/en/
  3. Select Earn -> My Claims.



  4. Select Connect My Wallet -> MetaMask.
  5. Select your Ledger account.



  6. Click Sign.



    Your Ledger device displays: Sign message.

  7. Using the buttons, verify the message that is displayed on your Ledger device. If all looks good, navigate to Sign message then press both button simultaneously to confirm.

    You Ledger account connects to Sandbox.



  8. Navigate to My Claims and find your Ledgerverse NFTs.
  9. Select Claim.



  10. Confirm the transaction in MetaMask.
    Your Ledger device displays: Review transaction.

  11. Review the transaction on your Ledger device and, if all looks good, select Accept and send.
  12. Press both buttons simultaneously to sign transaction.

    You're done! Once the transaction has been confirmed on the Ethereum network, your Ledgerverse NFTs will show in your Ethereum (ETH) account in Ledger Live.
