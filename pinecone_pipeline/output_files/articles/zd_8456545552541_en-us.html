Crypto.org Chain (CRO)

This article explains how to create a Crypto.org Chain (CRO) account with Ledger device.

Learn more about how to manage crypto assets with your Ledger device here.

 Looking for Cronos?

  • Crypto.org Chain and Cronos are two separate blockchain networks. Learn more
  • If you're looking to add a Cronos account to Ledger Live, check out this tutorial.

Before you start

  • Open Ledger Live, connect your Ledger device to My Ledger and make sure your device is running the latest firmware version.
  • If your Ledger device needs a firmware update, please update your Ledger Nano S / Nano S Plus / Nano X.
  • Ensure that you have the latest version of the Crypto.org Chain (CRO) app installed on your device. 

Add a Crypto.org Chain (CRO) account 

  1. In the left sidebar menu, go to Accounts.
  2. Click Add account.
  3. Choose Crypto.org (CRO) from the drop-down list.
  4. Click Continue.
  5. Connect your device to Ledger Live and unlock it. Ledger Live will start the process of creating your account.
  6.  Click Add account to finalize the process.
Receive CRO via Crypto.org
  1. In the left sidebar menu, click Receive.
  2. In the drop-down list, select CRO and click Continue.
  3. Connect your Ledger device to Ledger Live and unlock it.
  4. Select the Crypto.org Chain app on your device and press both buttons simultaneously to open it. The address will be displayed on your device and in Ledger Live.
  5. Verify that the address shown on your device matches the address displayed in Ledger Live.
  6. If they match, approve the address on your device—press the right button on your device until you see Approve. Click both buttons simultaneously for approval.
  7. Copy the address and share it with the sender. Ensure that the address does not change after you copy/paste it.
Send CRO via Crypto.org

You can send CRO from your account, using a Ledger device to verify and approve transactions.

  1. In the left sidebar menu, go to Send.
  2. From the dropdown menu, select your Crypto.org Chain account.
  3. Enter the recipient address. Double-check the address and click Continue.
  4. Enter the amount you want to send.
     If you're sending CRO to an exchange, you need to add a memo tag. Learn more



    You can find the memo in your Crypto.org account on the exchange. 

  5. Check the transaction summary—Amount, Network fees, and Total to debit—and click Continue.
  6. Confirm and approve the transaction on your Ledger device—press both buttons simultaneously to Approve. Press both buttons simultaneously to Accept and send the transaction.

The transaction is sent. Your account balance will be updated when the blockchain confirms the transaction.
