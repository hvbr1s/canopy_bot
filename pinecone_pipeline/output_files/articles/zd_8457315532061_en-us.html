Connect your Crypto.org Chain account to DeFi Wallet

Learn how to connect your Ledger Crypto.org Chain account to DeFi wallet to stake and manage CRO and Crypto.org Chain tokens.

Before you start

  • Open Ledger Live, connect your Ledger device to My Ledger and make sure your device is running the latest firmware version.
  • If your Ledger device needs a firmware update, please update your Ledger Nano S / Nano S Plus / Nano X.
  • Ensure that you have the latest version of the Ethereum (ETH) app installed on your Ledger device.
  • Also ensure that you have the latest version of the Crypto.org Chain (CRO) app installed on your Ledger device. 

Connect your Crypto.org Chain account to DeFi Wallet

  1. Go to https://crypto.com/eea/defi-wallet#defi_wallet_desktop
  2. Download and install the DeFi wallet.
  3. Connect your Ledger device to your computer and open the Crypto.org Chain app.
    Your Ledger device displays: Crypto.org ready
  4. Open the DeFi wallet, choose a password and select Create Wallet.




  5. Name your wallet.
  6. Select Want to create with hardware wallet?
  7. Leave the default settings and click Create Wallet.



  8. Click Connect.

  9. On your Ledger device, open the Ethereum (ETH) app.
    Your Ledger device displays: Application is ready.
  10. Click Connect.



  11. Click Continue
  12. Choose a password and click Encrypt wallet.



  13. Click Go to Home.

    You're done! Your Ledger Crypto.org Chain account and CRO balance will appear.
