View and download your invoice

You can download the invoice once your order has been shipped—the order status will change from Pending shipping to Completed. 

To view and download your invoice:

  1. Log in to the My Order page. To log in, you'll need order email address and reference number, which is displayed at the end of the checkout process and included in your order confirmation email. 
  2. Click My invoices in the left side menu. You'll see the information about your order.

  1. Click Download to get a PDF version of your invoice.

⚠️ Invoices are automatically generated when the order is paid and can't be modified afterward.
