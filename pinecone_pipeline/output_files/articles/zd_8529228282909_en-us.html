FEGtoken (FEG)

This article explains how to manage FEGtoken (FEG) with Ledger Live. FEGtoken is developed and supported by the FEGtoken Community. 

Before you start

  • Update Ledger Live through the notification banner or download the latest version.
  • Update the firmware of your Ledger Nano S / Nano S Plus / Nano X.

About FEGtoken (FEG)

FEGtoken (FEG) is the governance token of the FEG ecosystem—a fully decentralized trading ecosystem where holders can stake their tokens and earn passive income. 

 Correct network

Make sure to select the correct network of withdrawal on the exchange when sending assets to your Ledger accounts. FEGtoken currently operates on the Ethereum blockchain (ERC-20) and Binance Smart Chain (BEP-20). Though your BSC and Ethereum addresses look identical, it’s important to note that they exist on two different networks.

Selecting the wrong network may result in you not seeing the crypto assets in Ledger Live. Learn more

How do I use FEGtoken (FEG) with Ledger?

Ethereum (ERC-20) network

You can safely store your FEG tokens by sending them to your Ledger Ethereum address. It will show up within your Ethereum account.

 

 

Here is how to set up an Ethereum account in Ledger Live:

  1. Connect and unlock your Ledger device.
  2. Open Ledger Live and navigate to My Ledger.
  3. If asked, allow access to My Ledger by pressing both buttons on your Ledger device simultaneously. 
  4. In My Ledger, select the App catalog.
  5. Search for Ethereum and click Install.
  6. Add an Ethereum account. Learn how to do that here. 
  7. Generate the Ethereum receiving address you'd like to deposit the FEGtoken into. Learn more 

That's it! Be sure to have your Ethereum app open on your Ledger device when sending or trading FEG. 

Binance Smart Chain (BEP-20)

Another option is to send the FEGtoken via the Binance Smart Chain (BEP-20) network. You can do so by sending it to your Ledger BNB address. It will show up as a token underneath your BNB account.

  1. Connect and unlock your Ledger device.
  2. Open Ledger Live and navigate to My Ledger.
  3. If asked, allow access to My Ledger by simultaneously pressing both buttons on your device. 
  4. In My Ledger, search for Binance Smart Chain (BNB) and click Install.
  5. Navigate to Accounts and click Add account.
  6. Select Binance Smart Chain (BNB) and click Continue. 
  7. Navigate to the Receive tab, select your BNB account and click Continue. Ledger Live and your Ledger device will display your Binance Smart Chain address.
  8. Verify this address on your device and click Confirm. This address can be used to deposit your FEGtokens. 

    Be sure to have the Binance Smart Chain (BNB) app open when sending or trading FEGtoken.
