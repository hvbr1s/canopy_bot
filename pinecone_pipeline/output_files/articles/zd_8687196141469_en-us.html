How to redeem the gift card code and claim your BTC

This article explains how to redeem the gift card code and claim your BTC. This process applies to Black Friday 2022, Safe Journey offer, and Refer a friend gift cards.

You will receive the physical card for claiming BTC along with your Ledger device. The card will be inside an envelope. 

The steps for claiming your BTC are as follows:

  1. Gently scratch the gray area of the card to reveal the code.
  2. Open the Ledger Live app.
  3. Click the Refer a friend tab on the left-hand side of the app window.

  1. Create an account to access the dashboard. You can find the step-by-step instructions here -> How to join the Refer a friend.
  2. In the Refer a friend dashboard, click the Redeem your gift code tab. Then, select Redeem my gift code. 

  1. Sign in with your ETH account and follow the prompts on your device.
  2. Enter your gift code with the dashes.

You can claim your BTC after 30 days—during this time, you'll see the status of your balance as In Progress. 

Claiming your reward

You can claim your reward 30 days after receiving it. Please keep in mind that you have up to 1 year to claim it. The reward will be visible in your balance, and for referral rewards, they are in BTC. Ensure you have a BTC address to receive them.

  1. Click Claim.
  2. Add your new or existing Bitcoin account where the reward will be sent. 
  3. Follow the on-screen instructions to finalize the process.

Keep in mind that if you delete your Refer a Friend account, you won't be able to use the same ETH address to create a new one. If you have any unclaimed rewards on this account, they will be lost.
