Altura (ALU)

This article explains how to manage Altura (ALU) with Ledger Live. Altura is developed and supported by the Altura Community. 

Before you start

  • Update Ledger Live through the notification banner or download the latest version.
  • Update the firmware of your Ledger Nano S / Nano S Plus / Nano X.

About Altura (ALU)

Altura offers a powerful suite of tools and solutions for game developers looking to build, scale, and monetize their games using Web3 technology. Its user-friendly platform makes it easy for developers to harness the power of blockchain technology for their games.

 Correct network

Ensure that you select the appropriate network for withdrawal on the exchange when transferring assets to your Ledger accounts. For Altura, the Binance Smart Chain (BEP-20) network is the correct choice. Double-checking this step can prevent any potential errors and ensure that your assets are safely and correctly transferred.

Selecting the wrong network may result in you not seeing the crypto assets in Ledger Live. Learn more

How do I use Altura (ALU) with Ledger?

Binance Smart Chain (BEP-20)

  1. Connect and unlock your Ledger device.
  2. Open Ledger Live and navigate to My Ledger.
  3. If asked, allow access to My Ledger by simultaneously pressing both buttons on your device. 
  4. In My Ledger, search for Binance Smart Chain (BNB) and click Install.
  5. Navigate to Accounts and click Add account.
  6. Select Binance Smart Chain (BNB) and click Continue. 
  7. Navigate to the Receive tab, select your BNB account and click Continue. Ledger Live and your Ledger device will display your Binance Smart Chain address.
  8. Verify this address on your device and click Confirm. This address can be used to deposit your Altura (ALU). 

    Be sure to have the Binance Smart Chain (BNB) app open when sending or trading Altura (ALU). Also, you will also need a small balance of BNB to pay network fees when sending or trading Altura (ALU).
