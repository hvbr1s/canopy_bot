Is it safe to disconnect your Ledger device from Ledger Live?

This article answers the question “Is it safe to disconnect by unplugging a Ledger device from a computer/phone?”.
When it comes to disconnecting Ledger devices from their PC or smartphone, many users confuse them with USB flash drives.
The ''Safely remove hardware" guidelines, such as those for Windows or Mac, don't apply to Ledger devices. You can physically remove a Ledger device from your computer/smartphone without any special steps.

Note:

  • Unplugging a Nano S or Nano S Plus powers the device off.
  • Unplugging a Nano X, will not power it off but PIN-lock it.

When it comes to disconnecting a Ledger device from a smartphone via Bluetooth or USB, you can physically disconnect the cable from your device or, in case of a Bluetooth connection, turn the device off.

To avoid errors in Ledger Live, we still don't recommend disconnecting Ledger devices during transaction approvals or installation/uninstallation of Nano apps.
