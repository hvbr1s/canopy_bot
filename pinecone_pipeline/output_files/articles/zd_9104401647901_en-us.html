How to recover VET coins sent to your VeChain address via Binance Smart Chain

This tutorial describes how to recover VET coins sent to your Ledger VeChain address (obtained via Sync) on the Binance Smart Chain network. Check the VeChain wallet page to learn more. 

Before your start

  • Make sure your Ledger setup is completely up to date. Learn more.
  • Make sure to use Brave or Google Chrome browser.

Install the Eth recovery app on your Ledger device

  1. Open Ledger Live, navigate to Settings → Experimental features and activate Developer mode. 



  2. Navigate to My Ledger and install the Eth recovery app.



  3. Close Ledger Live.
  4. On your Ledger device, navigate to the Eth recovery app and press both buttons simultaneously to open it.
    Your Ledger device displays This is a recovery tool.

Find your VET account on Binance Smart Chain

  1. Go to MyCrypto.com and select Ledger.



  2. Select the Smart Chain network.



  3. Select Connect to Ledger Wallet then select your Ledger device and click Connect.



  4. Select add a custom derivation path.



  5. Fill in the label (VeChain) and custom path (m/44'/818'/0'/0) fields then click AddDPath.



  6. Back on the Select an account screen, make sure to select the VeChain derivation path you just added.



  7. Look for your VeChain address in the list, select it then click the Add Account button



  8. On the dashboard, scroll down to the Tokens section and click +Add.
  9. Fill in the following token details then click Add Token.

    Network: Smart Chain
    Address: 0x6FDcdfef7c496407cCb0cEC90f9C5Aaa1Cc8D888
    Symbol: VET
    Decimals: 18



    All done! Your VET balance should now show in the dashboard.

    It's recommended to move your VET tokens back to the exchange they came from. To do so please send a bit of BNB coins to the account that holds your VET then use the Send feature in MyCrypto.com to deposit your VET coins back to the exchange.

    Once your VET is back on the exchange, you can create a new withdrawal.

     Make sure to provide the Ledger VET address provided by Sync (learn more) and to select the VeChain network. Learn more about how to choose the correct network here.
