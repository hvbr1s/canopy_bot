Solve “connect ETIMEDOUT” error

This article explains how to solve the "connect ETIMEDOUT" error.

The error can happen because:

  • Your internet service provider is blocking access. In this case, please contact them directly for support.

    or

  • You're using a VPN that causes connections issues.  

Troubleshooting

  • If you're a VPN user, go to Settings > Experimental features, activate Experimental networking then try adding the account again.



    Not a VPN user?

  • Try using the mobile app instead. The Ledger Live mobile app is available for both iOS and Android devices and can be downloaded from the respective app stores.
  • If you are using a Wi-Fi network, try connecting to a different network or use a mobile hotspot instead.

Still stuck?

If the error persists, please get in touch with our Support team.
