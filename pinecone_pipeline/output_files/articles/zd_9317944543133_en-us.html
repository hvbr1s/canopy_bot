How to change language on your Ledger device

This article shows how to change the language on Ledger Nano X or Ledger Nano S Plus. For now, you can change between English, French, and Spanish.

Before you start

  • Make sure you updated the firmware on your Ledger devices: 
    • Nano X to firmware 2.1.0.
    • Nano S Plus to firmware 1.1.0.
  • You have the latest Ledger Live version.

Change the language

Desktop Mobile
  1. Open Ledger Live.
  2. Go to the My Ledger tab.
  3. Connect and unlock your Ledger device.
  4. On the top-right corner, click on English, next to Language.

  5. Select the language you want to set on your Ledger device and click Change language.

  6. Make sure your Ledger device is unlocked.
  7. Confirm the change on your Ledger device.
  1. Open Ledger Live.
  2. Go to the My Ledger tab.
  3. Make sure your Ledger device is connected to your phone via Bluetooth (Nano X) or via USB (Nano S Plus only on Android).
  4. On My Ledger tab, click on English, next to Language.

  5. Select the language you want to set on your Ledger device and click Change language.

  6. Make sure your Ledger device is unlocked.
  7. Confirm the change on your Ledger device.

Your Ledger device should have changed the language now.
