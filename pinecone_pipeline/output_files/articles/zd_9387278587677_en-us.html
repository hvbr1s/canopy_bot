Enabling price alerts in Ledger Live Mobile

This article explains how to enable price alerts for Ledger Live Mobile.

Ledger Live will notify you when the price of BTC or ETH rises/drops by 5% or more.

Enable/disable price alerts

  1. Open Ledger Live Mobile on your phone.
  2. Go to the Wallet tab.
  3. On the top corner click the Settings gear icon.
  4. Go to the Notifications section.
  5. Check the Large Mover setting.
  6. You can enable/disable it as you want.
