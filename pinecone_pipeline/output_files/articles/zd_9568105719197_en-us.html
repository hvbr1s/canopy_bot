How to troubleshoot email verification issues

Haven't received your verification email? Your verification link expired?

Find out how to fix email verification issues 👇

Follow these steps if you haven't received the verification email:

  1. Double-check the email you've entered to make sure it's correct.
  2. Check your spam folder—the emails can end up there. If that’s the case, add noreply@ledger.com to your allowlist and tap the link to verify your Ledger Recover login.
  3. Wait for one hour. Sometimes emails take longer to be delivered. 
  4. If none of this works, go to My Ledger → Ledger Recover and tap Didn’t receive it? Resend.

ℹ️ For now, it's not possible to change the email address. If you no longer have access to your email, you need to create a new Ledger Recover login, using a different email.
