How to cancel manual verification check

To cancel the manual verification check:

  1. Navigate to My Ledger in the Ledger Live app.
  2. Tap Ledger Recover.
  3. Enter your login.
  4. Tap Resume restore.
  5. Tap Try verifying again.

Your manual verification request will be canceled if you try verifying again.
