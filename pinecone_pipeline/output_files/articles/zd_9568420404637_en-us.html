How to reset your Ledger Recover password

This article explains how to reset the password for your Ledger Recover login. 

  1. Open the Ledger Live app.
  2. Go to My Ledger and access Ledger Recover.
  3. Tap Forgot password?

  1. Enter the email you’re using for your Ledger Recover login.
  2. You will receive an email with the password-reset link..
  3. Open the email and tap Reset password.
  4. Create and confirm a new password.
