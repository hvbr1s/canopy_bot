How to unsubscribe from Ledger Recover

To unsubscribe from Ledger Recover, you need to delete your login. That will automatically cancel your subscription.

You can unsubscribe from Ledger Recover anytime:

  1. Open the Ledger Live app and access Ledger Recover.
  2. Enter your email and password.
  3. Navigate to Manage subscription → Delete Ledger Recover login.

  1. You will receive an email with a link to unsubscribe. Open the email and tap the unsubscribe link.
  2. Enter your password to confirm that you want to delete your Ledger Recover login.
  3. Tap Delete my login.

You’ll receive an email confirming that you’ve unsubscribed from Ledger Recover.
