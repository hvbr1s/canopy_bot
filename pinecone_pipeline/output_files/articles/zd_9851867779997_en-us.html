Connecting your Filecoin (FIL) account to Glif.io

This article describes how to connect your Ledger Filecoin (FIL) account to Glif.io. Check the Filecoin wallet page to learn more.Â 

Before you start

  • Make sure to update Ledger Live to the latest version via this link.
  • Open Ledger Live and connect your Ledger device to My Ledger to ensure that your device is running the latest firmware version.
  • Ensure that the latest version of the Filecoin (FIL) app is installed on your Ledger device.

Connecting your Filecoin (FIL) account to Glif.io

  1. Go to https://www.glif.io/
  2. Connect your Ledger device to your computer and open the Filecoin app.
    Your Ledger displays: Filecoin Ready.



  3. Select Connect Wallet.



  4. Select Ledger (Filecoin).



  5. Select your Ledger device and click Connect.



    Your Ledger Filecoin (FIL) account appears.

  6. Click Close.



    You can now manage your Ledger Filecoin (FIL) account via Glif.io. Use the Send button to send FIL:



    You can find your Ledger deposit address in the dropdown menu in the top right corner of your screen.
